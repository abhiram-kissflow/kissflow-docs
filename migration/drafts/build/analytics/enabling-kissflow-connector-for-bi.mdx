## What is Kissflow connector for BI?

**Kissflow connector for BI** enables you to optimize your existing business operations by measuring and analyzing any qualitative and quantitative Kissflow data saved in Snowflake's cloud data warehouse using business intelligence tools such as Microsoft Power BI, Tableau, etc.

### Getting started

Kissflow connector for BI is set up on [Snowflake](https://www.snowflake.com/en/data-cloud/workloads/data-warehouse/), a data warehousing platform where your Kissflow account data for analytics is saved. You will be unable to make any changes to the data in Snowflake because it is all read-only. Using Snowflake as a connector, you may access and analyze this data using a Business Intelligence (BI) tool of your choice, such as [Microsoft Power BI](https://powerbi.microsoft.com/en-au/) or [Tableau](https://www.tableau.com/).
