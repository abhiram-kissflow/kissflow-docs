The board dashboard gives team members and managers a high-level view of work progress, deadlines, and workload distribution. It helps teams identify bottlenecks, monitor performance, get actionable insights, and make proactive decisions.

![](/migration-assets/board-dashboards/2.gif)

## Admin dashboard

Board admins and members can monitor overall team performance, upcoming deadlines, and workload trends using the following default widgets:

-   **AI insights**: AI analyzes your workflow data to uncover patterns and trends that aren’t easily visible in standard reports. These insights highlight opportunities for improvement, helping you make informed decisions and address bottlenecks.
    
-   **Workload distribution**: Visualizes how tasks are distributed across team members, helping identify over- or under-assigned workloads.
    
-   Due within 7 days
    
-   **Overdue in last 30 days**: 
    
    -   Tracks how often items exceed their due dates. 
        
    -   Shows the number of overdue items compared to all items with defined due dates.
        
-   Priority distribution
    

## My dashboard

As a board user (all board roles), you can track your personal progress and tasks with the widgets below.

-   **Notes awaiting my action**: Displays mentioned notes that require your response (reply or reaction).
    
-   Overdue tasks in last 30 days
    
-   Due within 48 hours
    
-   Items I'm watching
    

## AI-suggested widgets

Apart from the predefined default widgets, the dashboard suggests additional widgets and metrics tailored to your use case. These AI-powered recommendations help you discover relevant insights in the form of widgets.

You can add these widgets to your dashboard to create a personalized view that suits your daily workflow.
