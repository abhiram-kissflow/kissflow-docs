Kissflow plans:

✓ Basic ✓ Enterprise

This report is a graphical representation of the number of items against the item priority levels in your board. To access this report, click **Reports** on your board. This will take you to the **Metrics** tab by default, where you can find the priority summary report.

![](/migration-assets/priority-summary-report/5.gif)

You can further break down your report to quickly identify potential bottlenecks in your board. Click the **Break down** button (![](/migration-assets/priority-summary-report/1.svg)) to break down your report by assignee, status, requester, and custom fields. You can also choose to hide specific priority values from the report by clicking the **Show/hide** button (![](/migration-assets/priority-summary-report/2.svg)). Click the **More options** button (![](/migration-assets/priority-summary-report/3.svg)) to,

-   Copy the report as a PNG to the clipboard. 
-   Print the report.
-   Download the report as a PNG file.

For an enlarged view of the report, click the **Expand** button (![](/migration-assets/priority-summary-report/4.png)). Now, you will also have the option to set the time period of the report and apply filters to it apart from the above actions.You can click each bar on the report to get a list of items on the right side of your screen. From here, you can open items, reassign them, change their statuses and priorities, and delete them. 

![](/migration-assets/priority-summary-report/6.gif)
