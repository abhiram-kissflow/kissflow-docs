Sales is the engine of business growth, focused on converting leads into customers and nurturing long-term relationships. Integrations are the high-octane fuel for this engine, connecting CRMs like Salesforce and Salesmate with all other parts of the business—from marketing and e-commerce to project management and finance. By automating the flow of data, sales teams can eliminate manual tasks, gain a 360-degree view of the customer, accelerate the sales cycle, and spend more time doing what they do best: selling.

## Integration use cases with Kissflow

Here are practical, scenario-based examples of how Kissflow can trigger actions in Salesforce and Salesmate to solve common business problems.

### Salesforce

**Business goal**

**Trigger (in Kissflow)**

**Action (in Salesforce)**

**Detailed scenario**

**Key benefit**

Automate opportunity creation

A high-value wholesale order arrives via a Shopify webhook.

Create an opportunity.

Kissflow creates a new Opportunity in Salesforce, pre-filled with order value, product SKUs, and the reseller’s company name.

Ensures high-value B2B orders are captured as formal deals in the CRM without manual entry.

Maintain pipeline accuracy

A project manager checks off _Go-live complete_ in a progress tracker.

Update an opportunity.

The flow immediately updates the related opportunity, setting _Stage = Closed won_ and stamping the actual go-live date.

Improves sales forecasting accuracy by instantly reflecting project completion in the CRM.

Enable proactive support

An IoT sensor posts a critical fault message.

Create a case.

The workflow spins up a new case with priority = high, device ID, and customer location so support can act fast.

Automates service case creation from device alerts, enabling rapid response to critical issues.

Centralize field service

A field-techie logs a repair visit in a mobile app.

Update a case.

The integration updates the open case to _Status = Awaiting customer confirmation_ and attaches the service report link.

Keeps the central support case updated with the latest field service information.

Accelerate event lead capture

Staff submit badge scans through a form at a trade-show booth.

Create a contact.

Each submission creates a fresh contact in Salesforce with name, email, company, and an _Event = Expo 2025 tag_.

Instantly captures trade-show leads in the CRM, enabling faster sales follow-up.

Provide real-time risk alerts

Finance marks a customer as _past-due_ in a billing board.

Update a contact.

The integration flips the custom field _Billing\_Status_ to _Delinquent_ on the matching contact so CSMs see the risk.

Gives CSMs and reps critical, real-time risk alerts directly on the contact record.

Automate marketing lead capture

A webinar sign-up form converts.

Create a contact.

Instantly creates a new contact in Salesmate with name, email, and _Lead source = Webinar May 2025_.

Automates the capture of marketing-qualified leads directly into the sales CRM.

Manage customer lifecycle

A CSM marks _Onboarding complete_.

Update a contact.

The flow updates the existing Contact, setting the custom field _Customer\_Status = Active_ to switch playbooks from onboarding to expansion.

Keeps customer status accurate, enabling context-aware sales and expansion plays.

Centralize supplier data

Procurement approves a new supplier.

Create a company.

An integration step creates a company in Salesmate with address, VAT number, and industry, ready for sales engagement.

Makes supplier information accessible for sales and procurement processes.

Synchronize financial data

Finance changes a client’s credit limit.

Update a company.

The same value is pushed to the related company record in Salesmate for reps to see in real time.

Ensures sales reps have the most current financial context when negotiating deals.

Streamline _Quote-to-Deal_ Process

An e-commerce _Bulk quote_ form is approved.

Create a deal.

The app creates a new deal in Salesmate with amount, expected close, and pipeline = _Wholesale_.

Streamlines the process from customer price request to formal deal creation in the pipeline.

Automate pipeline progression

An implementation project hits 80 percent completion.

Update a deal.

The automation updates the corresponding Deal stage to _Negotiation won_ and probability = _100 percent_.

Provides real-time visibility into deal progression based on actual project milestones.

Ensure critical follow-up

A support ticket is escalated.

Create an activity.

Auto-creates a follow-up activity (_Call customer to confirm resolution_) assigned to the account manager in Salesmate.

Ensures critical support issues are handed off to the right person for follow-up via an actionable task.

Close the loop on tasks

A call outcome is logged.

Update an activity.

The integration updates the same activity to _status_ = c_ompleted_ with notes synced back.

Ensures a complete activity history in the CRM by syncing task outcomes.

Prevent duplicate data entry

A rep types an email in a _Call log_ form.

Find a contact.

The integration searches contacts; if a match is found, the form auto-populates the contact’s name and phone.

Saves rep time and prevents the creation of duplicate contacts by auto-populating forms.

Route documents intelligently

A project-handover app receives a project code.

Find a deal.

Kissflow searches deals by that external ID; if one open deal is returned, the workflow attaches a checklist and notifies the manager.

Automates the attachment of relevant documents to the correct deal, improving handovers.

Proactively manage daily tasks

A scheduled workflow runs each morning.

Boosts productivity by proactively delivering a daily to-do list to each rep.
