Agreement Management is the foundation of business operations, formalizing everything from hiring and sales contracts to procurement and legal approvals. Manual, paper-based signature processes are slow, costly, and lack security. Integrations are essential for modernizing this function, connecting core business workflows directly with trusted e-signature platforms like Adobe Sign and DocuSign.

This automation streamlines the entire lifecycle of an agreement, ensuring a fast, secure, and fully auditable process that accelerates business and enhances compliance.
