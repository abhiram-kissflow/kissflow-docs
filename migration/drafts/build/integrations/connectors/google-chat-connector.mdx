## What is Google Chat connector?

The Google Chat connector is an integration to enhance communication and collaboration within teams. It seamlessly integrates with Google Chat, offering a range of features to streamline interactions, automate workflows, and monitor progress.

## About Kissflow's Google Chat connector integration

Kissflow's Google Chat connector allows you to effortlessly integrate Google Chat with various third-party applications and Kissflow workflows. To integrate with Google Chat, you must have a valid Google Chat account and be a paid Kissflow customer.

If your workflow involves responding to specific events in Google Chat, such as sending messages or triggering automated processes, the Google Chat connector simplifies these tasks. By connecting Google Chat with other applications and workflows, you can automate actions and ensure a smooth workflow.

For now, the connector for Google Sheets does not support any trigger events.

### Google Chat connector as an action step

1.  In your Kissflow account, click the **Create** button in the left navigation panel and select **Integration** from the list. Enter a unique name and description for your integration and click **[Create](/t/60h4akx/creating-an-integration)**. 
    
2.  Once inside your editor, set up a trigger step. For example, you can use **Webhooks by Kissflow** as the trigger event to receive data from external applications.
    
3.  After setting up your trigger step above, click the **Add** button ({/* TODO: screenshot — source image expired (Google CDN); recapture from product UI */}) to to set up your action step. Search for **Google Chat** and select a desired action.
    
4.  After choosing your action event, authenticate the connector. Click **\+ Add an account** and sign in using your Google account. Kissflow uses Google's OAuth for authentication.
    
5.  After authenticating with your Google account, add relevant values against the Google Chat form fields. You can hit **Refresh fields** to view all the updated fields in the selected Google Chat form. You can also map Google Sheets fields from your trigger step or previous action steps. After the action is executed, these values will be automatically added to item fields.  
     
    
    ![](/migration-assets/google-chat-connector/1.png)
6.  After a successful configuration, test whether your Google Chat action step is set up correctly by clicking the **Test** button. The connector will pull field data from your selected Google Chat account and display it as a JSON output. Similar to trigger steps, errors are notified to you right away. Fix them before retesting the action step.
    

### Setting up a Google Chat connector

After creating a custom workflow with your Google Chat connector's action steps, switch **ON** the toggle button to activate the integration run. Once a trigger event occurs, the Google Chats’ actions are activated.

To ensure your integrations are properly configured and tested, review our [best practices guide](/t/p8yzsxn/best-practices-for-using-kissflow-integrations) for tips on building robust and maintainable solutions.
