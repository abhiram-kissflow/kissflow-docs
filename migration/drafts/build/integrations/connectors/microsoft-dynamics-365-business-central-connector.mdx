## What is Microsoft Dynamics 365 Business Central?

Microsoft Dynamics 365 Business Central is an all-in-one business management solution that helps organizations streamline processes, make informed decisions, and drive growth through finance, operations, sales, and customer service functionalities.

## About Kissflow's Microsoft Dynamics 365 Business Central integration

The Microsoft Dynamics 365 Business Central connector of Kissflow Integration facilitates the seamless connection and synchronization of Microsoft Dynamics 365 Business Central modules with various third-party apps and Kissflow apps. To integrate with Microsoft Dynamics 365 Business Central, users must possess a valid Microsoft Dynamics 365 Business Central account and be a paid Kissflow customer.  
  
An employee can submit a purchase request in a Kissflow Process, providing details such as the item description, quantity, preferred vendor, and additional notes. The request goes through several approval stages where managers can either approve or reject it. Once approved, the Kissflow Process connector automatically triggers the creation of a purchase order in Microsoft Dynamics 365 Business Central using the action Create a purchase order. The purchase order includes all the relevant information from the original request, such as the item description, quantity, vendor information, and specified notes.

## Configuring Microsoft Dynamics 365 Business Central connector

1.  In your Kissflow account, click the **Create** button in the left navigation panel and select **Integration** from the list. Enter a unique name and description for your integration and click **[Create](/t/60h4akx/creating-an-integration)**. 
    
2.  Once inside your editor, set up a trigger step, for example, a Kissflow Process trigger event, **When an item completes its workflow**.
    
3.  After setting up your trigger step above, click the **Add** button ( ![](/migration-assets/microsoft-dynamics-365-business-central-connector/1.png) ) next to set up your action step. Search for Microsoft Dynamics 365 Business Central and select an action event from the available options.
    
    ![](/migration-assets/microsoft-dynamics-365-business-central-connector/2.png)
4.  After choosing your action event, authenticate the connector. Click **+Add an account**. Kissflow uses Microsoft Dynamics 365 Business Central’s OAuth for authentication.
    
    ![](/migration-assets/microsoft-dynamics-365-business-central-connector/3.png)
5.  After authenticating with your Microsoft Dynamics 365 Business Central account, add relevant values against the Microsoft Dynamics 365 Business Central form fields. You can hit **Refresh fields** to view all the updated fields in the selected form. You can also map fields from your trigger step or fields from your previous action steps. These values will be automatically added to item fields after the action is executed.
    
6.  After a successful configuration, test whether your Microsoft Dynamics 365 Business Central action step is set up correctly by clicking the **Test** button. The connector will pull field data from your selected Microsoft Dynamics 365 Business Central account and display it as a JSON output. Similar to the trigger step, errors are notified to you right away. Fix them before retesting the action step.
    

### Setting up a Microsoft Dynamics 365 Business Central connector

After creating a custom workflow with your Microsoft Dynamics 365 Business Central connector's action steps, switch on the toggle button to activate the integration run. Once a trigger event occurs, the Microsoft Dynamics 365 Business Central action is executed.
