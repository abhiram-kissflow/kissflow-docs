You can validate a field against a certain set of conditions so that the form can only be submitted if the conditions are met.

Hover over a field in your form editor and click the grey tick icon (![](/migration-assets/field-validations/1.svg)) to access its validation settings or navigate to the **Validation** tab from the **Field properties** panel to your right.

![](/migration-assets/field-validations/2.png)
