As an application developer, you will have default access to forms that you create. To configure or change settings for the data form, click **Settings** on the data form editor.

## General settings

On the **General** tab, you can configure form titles.

### Configuring form titles

The form title is the unique name given to each entry in your data form. To configure form titles, click the **Add button (+)** and arrange default system fields or form fields in the order that you want, and then click **Save**. Based on this format, titles are auto generated and are displayed on top of each data form.

![](/migration-assets/data-form-settings/4.png)

## Security settings

On the **Security** tab, you can restrict flow and field permissions.

By default, all other flows in your account are able to find values in your data form. If you want to restrict this, select **Choose flows that can look up this data form** and type the flows you want to grant access to the data form. To remove a flow, click the **Remove** button next to the flow name. Click **Save** when finished.

You can also restrict which fields are able to be looked up.

You can also restrict the fields that can be looked up by other flows.

![](/migration-assets/data-form-settings/2.png)

## Public form settings

Under **Settings > Public form**, you can enable the data form to be a public form. By enabling the form to be a public form, a public form URL will be generated. This URL can be used for external sharing. Know more about public forms from [here](/t/35h9qk2/public-form).
