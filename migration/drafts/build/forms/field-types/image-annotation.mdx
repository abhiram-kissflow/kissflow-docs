## Image annotation in forms

Image annotation in forms across processes, boards, and apps allows you to add notes, explanations, or highlights to image attachments during the draft stage. Whether you want to emphasize key details, clarify specific elements, or provide additional context, this tool helps you ensure your image attachments communicate exactly what you intend.

To annotate an image attachment, click on the image, on the preview screen click **Annotate** on the right top corner.  
  
Annotation tools include:

-   **Selection tool:** Move or delete annotations.
    
-   **Pen tool:** Freehand drawing.
    
-   **Shapes:** Draw circles, rectangles, arrows, and lines.
    
-   **Highlighter:** Emphasize specific areas.
    
-   **Text:** Add notes or labels to images.
    
-   **Crop and rotate:** Trim unwanted parts or rotate image.
    
-   **Undo/Redo:** On the bottom center of the screen, to correct or revert actions.
    

Once you are done annotating, click **Save and replace** on the top right corner of your screen. Click **Clear all** if you want the original image back.

> **Note**  
> Image annotation is only available during the draft stage in forms across process, boards, and apps, but not available in dataforms in apps.
> 
> Once submitted, images can no longer be annotated.
