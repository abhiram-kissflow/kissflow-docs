## Inside apps

In the left panel of the editing environment, click the **Create** button (![](/migration-assets/creating-a-decision-table/1.png)), enter the name and description (optional), and click **Create**.

![](/migration-assets/creating-a-decision-table/4.gif)

### Renaming a decision table

To rename your decision table inside apps, click the **More options** button (![](/migration-assets/creating-a-decision-table/2.png)) near the **Update** button at the top right > **Rename**. You can change the description in the same way.

### Finding a decision table

You can find your decision table under the **Logic** tab in the left side panel.

![](/migration-assets/creating-a-decision-table/3.png)
