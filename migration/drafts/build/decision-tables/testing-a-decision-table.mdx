The test functionality of a decision table enables you to verify and ensure that it accurately evaluates all conditions and returns the corresponding results as expected.

To use test your decision table,

1.  Click the **Test** button in the top right.
    
2.  In the appropriate fields, enter the sample condition values to be tested.
    
3.  Click the **Run** button.
    

![](/migration-assets/testing-a-decision-table/1.png)

The decision table will evaluate the sample values and display the results of those corresponding conditions in a tabular format.
