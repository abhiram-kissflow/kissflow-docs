## Managing app settings in the app builder

The App builder comes with three different settings options. The primary one is the App settings. You will find **App settings** under the **Settings** icon within the app builder. App settings consists of the following setting options:

-   General settings
-   Security settings
-   Deployment history
-   Multilingual settings

General settingsUnder **General settings,** you can edit basic details of your app including the name, description, and logo.![](/migration-assets/settings-in-apps/1.png)

### Security settings

Under **Security settings**, you can manage what other apps have access to the data in your app through [app-to-app linking](/t/35yzjvd/application-to-application-lookup-through-linking).

### Deployment history

Get detailed deployment log about your app builds. You can also find details like:

-   Build version
-   Build number
-   Deployed date and deployer name
-   Status of the application

### Multilingual settings

[Multilingual settings](/t/y4yzjvb/multilingual-settings-in-apps) allow admins for Kissflow apps to customize their apps to be displayed in languages supported by the platform.
