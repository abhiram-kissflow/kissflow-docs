✕ Basic ✓ Enterprise

You can create an entire process workflow with the help of AI by clicking the **Suggest** button in the top panel. 

![](/migration-assets/creating-ai-suggested-workflow-and-steps/1.png)

A workflow suggestion based on the title of the process will be generated and displayed in a popup window. If you want to create the workflow based on a different keyword, click the text box in the top panel, enter the keyword, and click **Suggest**.In the popup window, you can choose to create the AI-suggested workflow or click **Refresh workflow** for another workflow suggestion.

![](/migration-assets/creating-ai-suggested-workflow-and-steps/4.gif)

For individual steps, click the **Add** button (![](/migration-assets/creating-ai-suggested-workflow-and-steps/2.png)) and click **AI-suggested steps**. Pick a suggestion or click **Refresh steps** for different suggestions.

![](/migration-assets/creating-ai-suggested-workflow-and-steps/3.gif)
