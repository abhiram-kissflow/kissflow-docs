## Renaming a page

1.  Open your app and click **Manage > Edit** to open the app builder.
    
2.  In the app builder, click **Pages**, select the page you wish to edit and open the page builder.
    
3.  Click the **More options** button (![](/migration-assets/renaming-duplicating-and-editing-pages/3.png)) present at the top-right of the page.
4.  Click the **Rename** option from the drop-down.
5.  In the following pop-up window, type a descriptive name and description for the page in the space provided next to **N****ame** and **Description**. 
6.  Click **Rename**.![](/migration-assets/renaming-duplicating-and-editing-pages/1.png)   

## Duplicating a page

1.   Open your app and click **Manage > Edit** to open the app builder.
    
2.  In the app builder, click **Pages**, select the page you wish to edit and open the page builder.
    
3.  Click the **More options** button (![](/migration-assets/renaming-duplicating-and-editing-pages/3.png)) present at the top-right of the page.
    
4.  Click the **Duplicate** option.
5.  In the following pop-up window, type a descriptive name for the page being duplicated  in the space provided next to the page name. 
6.  Click **Duplicate**.![](/migration-assets/renaming-duplicating-and-editing-pages/2.png)    

## Deleting a page

1.  Open your app and click **Manage > Edit** to open the app builder.
    
2.  In the app builder, click **Pages**, select the page you wish to edit and open the page builder.
    
3.  Click the **More options** button (![](/migration-assets/renaming-duplicating-and-editing-pages/3.png)) present at the top-right of the page and click the **Delete** option.
    
4.  In the **Delete page** window, click **Delete**_._

Alternatively, you can also rename, duplicate, delete a page from the left navigation menu:

1.  Click the **Interface** option in the left navigation menu
2.  Click the **more options** button beside the required page and you can choose one of the following actions from the pop-up menu: **Make as default, Rename, Duplicate, Delete**.
