## Installing an app

You install an app from the Kissflow App Store and start using it in your live account.

Note:

Only [Super Admins](/t/35h4ak036h4gg/user-roles-and-permissions) can install an app.

To install an app, 

1.  Sign in to your Kissflow account.
2.  Click the **Kissflow App Store** button (![](/migration-assets/using-kissflow-app-store/2.png)).
3.  Inside the App Store, hover over the free app you want to install. 
4.  You will find the **Install** button on the app’s card or its landing page. 
5.  Click **Install** > **Confirm**.

Once you confirm the installation of the app, the installation cannot be stopped. Installed apps can be accessed either through the Kissflow App Store or through the explorer.

Note:

You can install an app only once in an account. The Super Admin needs to delete and reinstall the app to install it again.

## Enquiring about an app

Many apps in the Kissflow App Store are provisional. Users can enquire about such apps, and Kissflow support staff will provide a personalized version of the app in their account. Follow the steps below to enquire about an app:

1.  Sign in to your Kissflow account.
2.  Click the **Kissflow App Store** button (![](/migration-assets/using-kissflow-app-store/2.png)).
3.  Hover over the app card with an **Enquire** button. 
4.  Click the **Enquire** button to access the enquiry form. 
5.  Fill out the enquiry form with information about the features you want for your app as well as the size of your team.![](/migration-assets/using-kissflow-app-store/1.png)
6.  Click **Submit**. Kissflow’s support staff will contact you regarding your enquiry.

> **Note:** Users can enquire only once for an app.
