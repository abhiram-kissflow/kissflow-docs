Receiving and acting on Kissflow notifications inside your organization’s chat application is easier with the **Kissflow Bot**. **Super Admins** can enable the Kissflow Bot for specific applications by navigating to **Account administration** > **Account settings** > **Bot settings**. Access Kissflow and receive real-time notifications right within your **Google Chat, Slack** or **Microsoft Teams** workspace and act on them without switching tabs. This integration informs you about your processes, and boards without switching platforms.

![](/migration-assets/bot-settings/1.png)

## Signing in to Kissflow from Google Chat

1.  Install the Kissflow app:
    
    1.  Go to your [Google Workplace Marketplace](https://workspace.google.com/marketplace?pann=ogb). 
        
    2.  Find the **Kissflow** app and install it.
        
        ![](/migration-assets/bot-settings/3.png)
        
2.  Now look for **Kissflow** in your Google chat list.  
     
    
3.  Sign in to your Kissflow account:
    
    1.  Type **/login** inside the Kissflow app within your Google Chat interface and press the **Enter/Return** key to sign in to Kissflow. A login URL will be generated automatically.
        
    2.  Click the login URL, enter your Kissflow account domain, and click **Submit**.
        
    3.  Click **Allow** in the permissions popup that appears and you’re all set.
        
        ![](/migration-assets/bot-settings/2.png)

Once signed in, you’ll start receiving notifications from your processes, and boards directly in the Kissflow app within your Google Chat interface. To sign out of the Kissflow app, type **/logout** and click the **Enter/Return** key.

## Signing in to Kissflow from Microsoft Teams

Once signed in, you’ll start receiving notifications from your processes, and boards directly in the Kissflow app chat within Microsoft Teams.

To sign out of the Kissflow app, enter **logout** and click the **Enter/Return** key.
