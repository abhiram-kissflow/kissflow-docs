As a Super Admin, you can control your account’s default settings that impact how data is formatted and displayed.

## **Account timezone**

Your organization's timezone is considered when you configure a scheduled action in integrations or include a [deadline](/t/p8h4a16/configuring-deadline) in a workflow. All flow data will be matched to your organization’s time zone. You can select a timezone using the map-based timezone picker or directly from the dropdown. The map can be zoomed in and out to see all available time zones worldwide.

![](/migration-assets/format-settings/1.png)

## **Language**

Choose the language for your Kissflow account. Kissflow is currently offered in English, Simplified Chinese, Italian, Korean, Japanese, Spanish, Brazilian Portuguese, French, German, Russian, Vietnamese, Thai, and Arabic.

## **Date format**

You can choose a date format of your choice. This will adjust the formatting of your account's number, date, and datetime fields. Here are the date format options available,

-   Local (based on your browser settings)
-   US (D/M/YYYY)
-   European (MM/DD/YYYY)
-   ISO (YYYY-MM-DD)

## **Number and currency format**

Local number and currency conventions are automatically discovered from the browser settings. By default, numbers will always automatically be formatted based on the user's local conventions.

> **Note:**
> 
> An individual user's preferences can override the organization's timezone, date and time format, and language.

Click **Save** when you are done.
