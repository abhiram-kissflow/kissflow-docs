## Managing subscription

Super Admins and Billing Admins can access an account’s subscription details by clicking **Account administration** > **Subscription & billing**. You can know the current status of your plan, and number of user licenses that can be availed as part of your plan in this section.

![](/migration-assets/subscription-and-billing/1.png)

You can also contact our support executives for account-related queries.

> **Note:**  
> **Super Admins** and **Billing Admins** have access to this page. Learn more about [roles in Kissflow](/t/35h4ak0/user-roles-and-permissions).

## Invoice details

Go to **Account administration** > **Subscription & billing** > **View invoice** to view and download your invoice. 

![](/migration-assets/subscription-and-billing/2.png)
