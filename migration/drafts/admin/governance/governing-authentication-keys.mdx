Governing authentication keys is crucial for several reasons. First and foremost, it helps maintain the security and integrity of your Kissflow account and its data. As a [Super Admin, or an IAM Admin](/t/35h4ak0/user-roles-and-permissions), you can control who has access to your APIs and integrations by managing and governing these keys. This minimizes the risk of unauthorized access or data breaches. Proper governance of authentication keys also allows for better tracking and auditing of API.You can learn about all the authentication keys (access and API keys) in your account on a single page. Details like expiry of the keys, active time, created at and by, last accessed time, and their statuses are displayed so you can have proper visibility.![](/migration-assets/governing-authentication-keys/1.png)
