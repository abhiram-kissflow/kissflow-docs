## What is account governance?

Account governance is a centralized hub, allowing [Super Admins and IAM Admins](/t/35h4ak0/user-roles-and-permissions) to audit and manage their Kissflow accounts effortlessly. This feature is pivotal for maintaining data security and protecting sensitive information within your account. A user-friendly dashboard gives you a comprehensive view of all users, groups, workflows (including processes and boards), datasets, lists, connections, and authentication keys associated with your Kissflow account.

## Benefits of account governance

-   **Compliance and standards:** By implementing robust governance practices, you can maintain data integrity and establish a responsible and compliant operations framework.
    
-   **Data protection and risk mitigation:** Account governance is crucial in safeguarding sensitive proprietary data and critical assets. By preventing risks associated with ungoverned data, you can mitigate vulnerabilities and maintain the security and integrity of your account’s data.
    
-   **Activity monitoring and abnormal detection:** By tracking account activities over time, you can gain valuable insights and identify any abnormalities or suspicious behavior. This proactive approach allows for timely intervention, ensuring the security and integrity of your account.
