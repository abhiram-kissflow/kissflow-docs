A [form](/t/h7h4azm/creating-a-form) is an entity used to collect input from participants in your workflow. It is used in [processes](/t/h7h4a18/process-overview), [boards](/t/q6h9ql3/board-overview), and [dataforms](/t/35yzjag/creating-and-managing-dataforms) in Kissflow. A form consists of three main components: [section](/t/h7h4azm/creating-a-form#sections), [field](/t/h7h4azm/creating-a-form#fields), and [table](/t/h7h4azm/creating-a-form#child-tablesnbsp).
