While smart attachments autofills process form fields, [guided autofill](/t/m1yzcgv/guided-autofill-for-smart-attachments) allows you to further instruct **how to autofill**.  
This can be done by adding autofill instructions to form fields. You can instruct AI to follow a format or template or even translate the autofill content to a desired language.
