[Account governance](/t/x2hvt75/auditing-and-governance-in-kissflow) under account administration is a centralized hub, allowing [super admins](/t/35h4ak0/user-roles-and-permissions#super-admin) and [IAM admins](/t/35h4ak0/user-roles-and-permissions#iam-admin) to audit and manage their Kissflow accounts. This feature is pivotal for maintaining data security and protecting sensitive account information.
