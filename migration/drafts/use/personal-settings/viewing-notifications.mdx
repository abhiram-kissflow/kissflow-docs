You can find all of your notifications by clicking the **bell** icon ( ![](/migration-assets/viewing-notifications/5.svg) ) in the upper-right corner of the screen.

![](/migration-assets/viewing-notifications/1.png)

From this pop-up, you can scroll through all of your notifications sorted by the most recent. New notifications are highlighted, and the number shows by the button. When you click a notification card, you will go to the page associated with the notification.

![](/migration-assets/viewing-notifications/2.png)

To mark an individual notification as read, click the grey-filled circle (.), which appears on the right side when you hover over it. If you click **Mark all as read**, all the unread notifications will be marked as read.

Click the **Settings** button (![](/migration-assets/viewing-notifications/4.svg)) to manage notification preferences for all the flows you are a part of. 

Click **Show all** to go to the full-screen notifications page, where you can see previews of the items. From the notifications page, you can click a tab to see **All**, **Unread**, **Mentions**, and **Action needed** notifications.

![](/migration-assets/viewing-notifications/3.png)

When you click a particular notification card, you will see the feed card details in the preview section on the right.
