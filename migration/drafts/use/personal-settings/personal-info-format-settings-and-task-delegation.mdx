## Accessing your profile

Click your profile picture in the upper-right corner of the Home screen and click **My settings**. The **My settings** page has three categories: **General**, **Security**, and **Notification settings**. The **General settings** page includes **Personal info**, **Format settings**, and **Task Delegation**.

![](/migration-assets/personal-info-format-settings-and-task-delegation/4.png)

## Personal info

The Personal info page allows you to fill in your details, upload a profile picture, add a signature, and manage your email address. You can also change your password if it is compromised or your company requires a password change. If you forget your current password, follow the steps below:

1.  Click **Change password** > **Forgot password**?
2.  Click **Send email**. You will receive an email with instructions on how to reset your password. This email is a time-sensitive email that expires after 24 hours. 

To change your password, click **Change password**. Enter your current password and your new password, and click **Save**.

## Format settings

You can manage the format settings to format the display and data of your account. These settings apply to your account, not the account-wide format settings, which only the [Super Admin](/t/35h4ak0/user-roles-and-permissions#super-admin) can change. 

**Account t****ime zone**. Your organization's time zone is considered when you configure a scheduled action for integration or include a deadline in a workflow. All data from processes, boards, and apps will match your time zone.

**Language**. Choose the language for your Kissflow account. Kissflow is currently offered in English, French, Spanish, German, Italian, Russian, Korean, Simplified Chinese, Japanese, Vietnamese, Thai, Arabic, Polish, and Portuguese.

**Date format**. Choose a suitable date format for your business. The number and currency format are based on your browser settings. Remember to click **Save** after choosing your preferences.

![](/migration-assets/personal-info-format-settings-and-task-delegation/1.png)

## Task delegation

You can automatically delegate process tasks to another user when you are going to be unavailable for a certain period of time. Your existing items will stay with you. You can also cancel the delegation at any time. 

Click **Add delegation** to enable task delegation, and select a user under **Who do you want to delegate your tasks to?** Specify how long you want your items to be delegated to the selected user. 

![](/migration-assets/personal-info-format-settings-and-task-delegation/2.png)

> **Note:**
> 
> Existing process items in your queue will not be delegated. New process items alone will start to be redirected to the delegatee.

### Modifying the delegation information

To modify the details of the user to whom your items have to be delegated, click the **Edit delegation** button beside the user's name. If you want to cancel the task delegation, click **Cancel delegation** and let Kissflow assign items to you as usual. You can regain control over all your items as before. However, all previously delegated items will still remain with the delegated user.

![](/migration-assets/personal-info-format-settings-and-task-delegation/3.png)

> **Note:**
> 
> [Super Admins can override any delegation](/t/60h4ak1) set by a user under **My settings.** They cannot override a delegation that has been already overridden but can edit it.
