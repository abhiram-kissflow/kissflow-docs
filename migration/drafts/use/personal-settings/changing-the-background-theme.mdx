Kissflow plans:

✓ Basic ✓ Enterprise

You can change the theme of your account to **Light**, **Dark**, or **Dim** mode from the **Themes** section. Click your profile picture on the upper right corner of the page > **Themes**. Choose your preferred theme and click **Done**.

![](/migration-assets/changing-the-background-theme/1.png)

-   **System default**: Uses the default theme of the local machine
-   **Light**: White background with dark text
-   **Dark**: Black background with light text
-   **Dim**: Grey background with light text

> The theme you select will be applied to all the Kissflow accounts accessed through the browser on which you set the theme.
