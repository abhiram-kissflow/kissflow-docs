The application dashboard has the following two widgets:

-   My tasks
-   My items

## My tasks

A task is an item that awaits approval or any other action from you. You can view all your ongoing and overdue tasks with the help of the **My tasks** widget on the **Home** page and add filters based on due dates if you wish. You can also click **Show all** in the **My tasks** section to get a detailed list of your tasks.

### Managing approvals

1.  Tap the **My tasks** widget in the dashboard.
    
2.  Tap a task from the list.
    
3.  Tap **Approve** if you would like to approve the item.
    
4.  Tap **More actions** if you would like to perform some other action on the item. You can now reject, send back, reassign, or withdraw the item as necessary.
    
    ![](/migration-assets/managing-processes-in-pwa/1.gif)

### Other actions

1.  Tap the **My tasks** widget in the dashboard.
    
2.  Tap a task card from the list.
    
3.  Tap the **More options** menu in the upper-right corner of the task.
    
4.  Tap **Progress** to view the workflow and the current status of the task.
    
5.  Tap the **Comments** button (![](/migration-assets/managing-processes-in-pwa/2.png)) to leave a comment about the task.
    
    ![](/migration-assets/managing-processes-in-pwa/3.gif)

> **Tip:**
> 
> If there isn't any task assigned to you yet, you can always stay updated by subscribing to the app notifications. Learn more on [how to manage your email and push notifications here](/t/p8h9qzc63h4g3/android-devices#enabling-push-notificationsnbsp).

## My items

The items you initiated will be displayed here, and you can filter them based on status and creation timeline.

1.  Tap the **My items** widget in the dashboard.
2.  Tap an item card from the list.
3.  Tap **Withdraw** or **Reassign** based on your preference.
4.  Tap the **More options** menu in the upper-right corner of the item to view its progress or duplicate it as necessary.
