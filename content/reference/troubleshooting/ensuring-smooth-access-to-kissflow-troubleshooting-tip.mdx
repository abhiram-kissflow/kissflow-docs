# Ensuring smooth access to Kissflow

If you can't reach Kissflow, or parts of the product fail to load, the most common causes are a corporate firewall blocking required domains, or an ISP blocking the underlying Amazon or Google infrastructure Kissflow runs on.

## Symptom: Kissflow won't load, or loads partially

Pages fail to load, styling or icons are missing, or features like chat support, file picker, or maps don't work — while other websites work fine.

**Cause:** Your organization's firewall is blocking one or more domains that Kissflow depends on.

**Fix:** Ask your IT team to whitelist the following domains.

### Core application

- `https://*.kissflow.com` — the asterisk (`*`) whitelists any subdomain of `kissflow.com`, which covers your account's own subdomain (for example, `https://yourcompany.kissflow.com`). If your firewall doesn't support wildcard entries, whitelist your exact account subdomain instead.
- `https://static-cdn1.kissflow.com` — static assets.
- `https://www.gstatic.com` — subresources for Google-hosted embeds like reCAPTCHA and Google Maps.

<Callout type="info">
  Some accounts are served additional static-asset domains depending on your data region (for example, a second CDN host for US accounts, or a `kissflow.eu` variant for EU accounts). If assets still fail to load after whitelisting the domains above, check with your Kissflow contact for the exact CDN domain for your account's region.
</Callout>

### Supporting services

| Domain | Used for |
| --- | --- |
| `https://o300529.ingest.sentry.io` | Error reporting |
| `https://widget.intercom.io` | In-app chat support |
| `https://js.intercomcdn.com`, `https://api-iam.intercom.io`, `wss://nexus-websocket-a.intercom.io` | In-app chat support (Intercom-hosted resources) |
| `wss://*.firebaseio.com` | Real-time updates |
| `https://fonts.gstatic.com`, `https://fonts.googleapis.com` | Fonts |
| `https://maps.googleapis.com` | Map fields |
| `https://www.googleapis.com` | Google Drive file picker |
| `https://unpkg.com` | Custom low-code components |
| `https://www.recaptcha.net` | Bot protection on forms |
| `https://*.storage.googleapis.com` | File and image storage |
| `https://us.i.posthog.com` | Product analytics |

<Callout type="info">
  If you previously whitelisted `https://app.posthog.com` for product analytics, update it to `https://us.i.posthog.com` — Kissflow now sends this traffic through PostHog's regional ingestion host.
</Callout>

If your account uses the Kissflow App Store, also check with your IT team whether `appstore.us.kissflow.store` and `resource.us.kissflow.store` need to be whitelisted separately, since these can be served from a different environment than your main account domain.

## Symptom: Kissflow is unreachable even with domains whitelisted

**Cause:** Your ISP may be blocking the underlying Amazon or Google cloud infrastructure that Kissflow's servers run on, rather than blocking Kissflow's domains directly.

**Fix:** Try accessing Kissflow over a VPN. If Kissflow loads normally through the VPN, ask your ISP to unblock Amazon and Google server ranges, or continue using a VPN as a workaround.
