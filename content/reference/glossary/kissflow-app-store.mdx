[Kissflow App Store](/t/60h9qts/introduction-to-kissflow-app-store) is a centralized repository that hosts a wide array of applications that cater to different business use cases. Unlike the process and board templates in Kissflow’s template gallery, the Kissflow App Store hosts pre-built applications that are designed and developed by Kissflow specialists with little to no coding effort. After installation, you can quickly deploy these applications for your end users. You can also inquire for new apps that address use cases that are not already available.
