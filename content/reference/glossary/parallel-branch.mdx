[Parallel branches](/t/m1h9qmv/making-a-process-workflow) in workflows enable concurrent tasks or conditional routing based on form data, ensuring flexibility and efficiency in process design.The workflow won't move forward until all parallel branches are completed. This feature is particularly useful for processes where multiple teams need to act simultaneously without depending on each other's tasks.
