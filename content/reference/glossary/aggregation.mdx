[Aggregation field](https://community.kissflow.com/t/83h4azk/aggregation-field) pulls together data from various fields to show relationships and perform calculations. You can use it to calculate sums, averages, or other aggregations of data within a single form or across different processes. You can apply filters to restrict the values used in the aggregation, giving you more control over the results.
