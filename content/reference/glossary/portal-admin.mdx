A [Portal Admin](/t/p8h4atv/administering-portals) has access to the [Portal](/t/m1h4azh/portal-overview) settings option under Portal administration. Portal admins can control the portal's default settings that impact how data is formatted and displayed. They will also have access to the user management screen, audit log, and deployment history.
