[Model viewer](/t/60yzjcv/model-viewer) in Apps is similar to an entity-relationship (ER) model widely used in DBMS (Database Management System). Model viewer can help you create a real-time visualisation of all the process, board, data forms that are present in the app and the relationship between them.
