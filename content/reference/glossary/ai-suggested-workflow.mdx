[AI-suggested workflow](/t/60hv9mr/creating-ai-suggested-workflow-and-steps) is a feature that automatically generates an entire process workflow based on the process title or a keyword you provide. When you click Suggest, our AI analyzes your input and creates a complete set of workflow steps, saving you time and effort in process design. This builds upon our AI-suggested fields feature, offering a more comprehensive solution for workflow creation.
