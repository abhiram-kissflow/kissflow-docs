A solution in Kissflow refers to any business use case or an operational need solved using the platform’s no-code tools, such as a **[Process](/t/h7h4a18/process-overview)**, **[Board](/t/q6h9ql3/board-overview)**, **[App](/t/x2h9qkj/introduction-to-kissflow-apps)**, or **[Portal](/t/m1h4azh/portal-overview)**. 

Solutions can function as standalone entities (for example, a single process or board) or as integrated systems, where multiple solutions and even external tools work together to deliver a unified outcome.
