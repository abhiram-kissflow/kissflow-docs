**[Kissflow App Builder](/t/m1yzj9p/app-builder-overview)** is a workspace within the development environment where you can assemble the pages and components of your app to create a complete, functional application. It is accessible only from the development environment. That is, when you click **Edit app** on a published or unpublished app, the app builder automatically opens.

The app builder makes it easy to develop multiple features simultaneously, allowing you to work on a page while also modifying a process in a separate tab, accessible from the left panel. You can switch between tabs without losing any progress. It also provides several default components that you can simply drag and drop onto your canvas, along with a variety of layout and styling options to customize the look and feel of your app.
