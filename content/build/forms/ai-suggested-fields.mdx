**AI-suggested fields** are form fields generated using Artificial Intelligence (AI), a technology that processes and infers large data through Machine Learning (ML). These fields are suggested to you based on the form name you enter. 

Unlike other types of fields, which need to be configured, the AI-suggested fields are populated with the relevant field name and field type. For example, if you’re creating a board for tracking bugs, some fields you may require are _Bug summary_, _Bug classification_, and so on. You may drag-and-drop a text field from the list of basic fields for _Bug summary_ and a dropdown field for _Bug classification_ and configure them. Instead, the AI-suggested fields assist you by suggesting the fields you may require for the bug tracking board and deciding their field name and type.

![](/migration-assets/ai-suggested-fields/1.png)

## Accessing AI-suggested fields

1.  Sign into Kissflow.
    
2.  Create a Process/Board/Dataform (Apps) of your choice.
    
3.  You'll find **AI-suggested fields** in the left side fields panel, next to **All fields**.
    
4.  Click the **AI-suggested fields** tab to pick the required fields for your form.
    

## Using AI-suggested fields

-   Drag and drop the fields required for your form from the fields list.
    
-   Use the **Showing suggestions for** field to get alternative field suggestions. Enter a different name and click **Get suggestions** to view results.
    

![](/migration-assets/ai-suggested-fields/2.png)

> **Note:**
> 
> The alternative name that you provide can be a different form name for which you’d like to receive AI suggestions. You may add a generic name to your form to get apt suggestions instead of specific names used within your organization.

-   Click **Refresh fields**(![](/migration-assets/ai-suggested-fields/3.png)) to get additional suggestions.
