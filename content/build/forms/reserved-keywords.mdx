**Reserved keywords** are terms or phrases appropriated for special use that may not be used in the creation of variable names, that is, these words or phrases cannot be used to name processes, boards, tables, fields, or anything that requires the user to input a name.

Here is a complete list of **Reserved keywords** that cannot be used in any Kissflow workflow.
