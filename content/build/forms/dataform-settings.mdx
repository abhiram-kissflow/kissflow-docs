As an app developer, you will have default access to forms that you create. To configure or change settings for the dataform, click **Settings** on the dataform builder.

![](/migration-assets/dataform-settings/1.png)

## General settings

In the **General** **settings** tab, you can configure form titles.

### Configuring form title

The form title is the unique name given to each entry in your dataform. To configure form titles, click the Add button (+) and arrange default system fields or form fields in the order that you want, and then click **Save**. Based on this format, titles are auto generated and are displayed on top of each dataform.

![](/migration-assets/dataform-settings/2.png)

## Public form settings

**Public form settings**, you can enable the dataform to be a public form. By enabling the form to be a public form, a public form URL will be generated. This URL can be used for sharing the form outside your organization. You can customize public forms using the **Customize** option. Learn more about public form customization [here](/t/q6yzjas/public-form).

![](/migration-assets/dataform-settings/3.png)

> **Note:** CSV import is not supported on public forms on any device (desktop or mobile). This is because public forms are often embedded on external sites, and Kissflow cannot securely verify the data source or support the backend requirements for bulk uploads. Therefore, CSV import is disabled for public forms to ensure system security.
