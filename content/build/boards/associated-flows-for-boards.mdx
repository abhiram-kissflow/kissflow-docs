The **Associated flows** section helps you understand how your board interacts with other flows. They offer a visual map of dependencies, making it easier to manage changes.

Click the **Manage** button at the top right and click the **Associated flows** button.  
 

![](/migration-assets/associated-flows-for-boards/1.png)

   
There are two types of associations: **Looked up flows** and **Connected flows**.

## 1\. Looked up flows

[Lookup fields](/t/g9h4azr/lookup-field) allow a board to pull and display data from other flows.  
 

![](/migration-assets/associated-flows-for-boards/2.png)

Under **Looked up flows**, you can view these relationships:

### a. Flows looked up to

This section shows which flows are looking up this board to fetch and display information.

**Example**: If another board needs to reference details like **IT asset availability** or **Vendor Names** from the current board, it would appear under **Flows looked up to**.

### b. Flows looked up from

This section displays the flows that the current board is looking up.

**Example**: If your **IT Asset approval** board is pulling **Asset Types** or **Asset details** from a master dataset, the master list flow will appear under **Flows looked up from**.

You can [configure lookup](/t/83ymdnx/security-settings-for-flows) settings directly from this page if you need to update or remove associations.
