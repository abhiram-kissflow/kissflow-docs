You can archive and delete a board when you no longer need it. A board can only be deleted after it is archived. When you archive a board, all the contents will no longer be accessible, and no one can add new items to it. 

## Archiving and unarchiving a board

To archive a board, click the **More options** button (![](/migration-assets/archiving-deleting-and-duplicating-a-board/2.png)) at the top right > **Archive.**

However, if you are an admin of the board, you can access the audit log for the board and unarchive or delete it.

To find the archived boards you once managed, click **Explore** from the left panel and select the **Managed by me** tab at the top. To view the archived boards managed by others, select the **Others** tab. Select the **Boards** tab in the left navigation panel and select the status as **Archived**.

![](/migration-assets/archiving-deleting-and-duplicating-a-board/1.png)

### [Unarchiving a b](/t/y4h9qtb/publishing-editing-duplicating-archiving-and-deleting-a-process#unarchiving-a-process)oard

To unarchive a board you managed, click **Explore** > **Managed by me** > **Board** > **Status - Archived**. To unarchive other boards, click **Explore** > **Others** > **Board** > **Status - Archived**. Hover over a board and click the **Unarchive** button ( ![](/migration-assets/archiving-deleting-and-duplicating-a-board/3.png) ) to make the board available again. 

## Deleting a board

To permanently delete a board and all its data, find the board inside **Explorer**, hover over it, click the **More options** button (![](/migration-assets/archiving-deleting-and-duplicating-a-board/2.png)), and click **Delete**.

## Duplicating a board

If you want to create a board that is identical to an existing one, you can duplicate it. Duplicated boards will have the same settings and configurations as the original ones. However, items from the original board are not duplicated.

1.  On your board, click the **More options** button (![](/migration-assets/archiving-deleting-and-duplicating-a-board/2.png)) > **Duplicate**.
2.  Enter a name for the duplicate board in the **New name** field. The default name of the new board will be _Copy of &lt;original board name>_.
3.  Enter a new ID prefix for generating item IDs.
4.  Click **Duplicate**.![](/migration-assets/archiving-deleting-and-duplicating-a-board/4.png) 

> **Note:**
> 
> While duplicating a board, its associated integrations will not be duplicated. You'll have to [duplicate those integrations](/t/35h4akt/duplicating-archiving-and-deleting-an-integration) manually.
