Reports help you derive insights into how your board is functioning. You can generate customized reports to analyze trends and various metrics about your workflow.

You can access custom reports by clicking the **Reports** button in the upper-right corner of the board and then clicking the **Custom reports** tab. Only [admins](/t/m1h9qls/managing-members-and-roles-boards) can create reports. Members and initiators can only access reports admins have shared with them.

## Creating a new report

To create a new report, click **\+ Create report**. Choose the type of report, provide a name for the report, and click **Create**.

### Types of reports

-   [Tabular reports](/t/h7h9qld/tabular-reports-boards) display information in columns and rows, like a spreadsheet. 
-   [Charts](/t/p8h9qlw/charts-boards) display data graphically in one of ten chart types, including area, bar, doughnut, line, pie, and bubble chart.
-   [Pivot tables](/t/35h9ql2/pivot-tables-boards) can be used to summarize large quantities of data quickly. You can convert columns into rows and rows into columns.![](/migration-assets/overview-of-custom-reports-in-boards/1.png) 

### The All Items report

The first time you open the **Reports** page, you’ll see an auto-generated report called _&lt;board\_name> All Items_. This is a tabular report that shows every item in your board. It cannot be deleted or renamed, but you can configure it to change how data is displayed.

### Finding other reports

Use the **Search** box to find any report by name quickly. Type in the name of a report to get relevant results. You can use the **Sort** button to rearrange your reports based on their last modified time, name, or created date.
