[Kissflow Analytics](/t/m1h9qzd/introduction-kissflow-analytics) Reports offers a robust solution for generating comprehensive reports by merging diverse data sources such as process tables, board tables, child tables, system tables, datasets, and [dataviews](/t/83h9qzn/understanding-dataviews). This tool allows for a high degree of customization, enabling the creation of meaningful reports through SQL commands. It facilitates the conversion of complex data into accessible charts and tables. Kissflow Analytics empowers users to analyze metrics, identify significant data patterns, and support data-driven decision-making by providing custom reports.

## Types of reports

There are three different types of reports in Analytics.

1. **[Tabular report](/t/g9h9qzb/tabular-reports)**: Displays your data in rows and columns, forming a grid similar to a spreadsheet.
2. **[Chart report](/t/h7h9qzr/chart-reports)**: Displays data graphically in one of the ten chart types, including **area**, **bar**, **doughnut**, **line**, **pie**, and **scatter** chart.
3. **[Pivot tables](/t/q6h9qz5/pivot-reports) or Pivot report**: Quickly summarizes large quantities of data. You can convert columns into rows and rows into columns.

## What can you do with reports in Kissflow Analytics?

-   Create **dataviews** to combine data from multiple Kissflow forms that provide insights about the data you want to visualize.
-   **[Style](/t/y4h9qzg/using-reports#applying-styles-to-your-report)** your reports according to your choice. 
-   Customize your report by adding **filters** and using the **sort** options to suit your requirements.
-   Create various **[chart types](/t/h7h9qzr/chart-reports#types-of-charts)**, including pie charts, bar charts, and line charts. 
-   Use [drilldown reports](/t/y4h9qzg/using-reports#enabling-drilldown-in-reports) to access underlying data in your chart and pivot reports.
-   **Share** reports by setting various **[permission levels](/t/p8h9qz2/managing-report#managing-roles-and-permissions)** to control who has access to the data. 
-   **Export** your reports in different formats, such as CSV or JSON, making it easier to share with other stakeholders.
