You can determine what action the assignee and the initiator of an item can take at each step.

Go to the **Permissions** tab in the process editor. Select any step and scroll down to the bottom of the form. Click **Configure** **actions** and hover over the actions.

![](/migration-assets/configuring-actions-for-process-form-steps/1.png)

##### Actions for new items

When a user with the role to access the process form initiates a new item, they have two actions at the bottom. By default, these say **Submit** and **Discard**. 

![](/migration-assets/configuring-actions-for-process-form-steps/2.png)

To configure this, click the step called **Start** and scroll to the bottom. You can choose to **Hide** or **Show the Discard action** to the initiator by default. The **Submit** button cannot be hidden because it’s the action that  moves the item to the next step and must be shown.

If you want to change the wording, hover over either **Discard** or **Submit** and click **Advanced**. You can change the name to another option or click **Custom** to enter a new name.

![](/migration-assets/configuring-actions-for-process-form-steps/3.png)

In the **Advanced settings**, you can also add a validation based on the data in any field. The field validations you configured in the form will also be in force, but this is a good option in case those values are dynamic throughout the workflow or if you want to use a system field.

##### Assignee actions for items in progress

At each step you can configure what actions can be taken by the assignee.

![](/migration-assets/configuring-actions-for-process-form-steps/6.png)

Choose any step on the left menu. Scroll down the form and click **Configure actions**.   

-   **Submit** is the action that advances the workflow to the next step.
-   **Reassign** allows the current assignee to assign the item to a different user in your app You can also restrict reassignment to specific roles by toggling the **Restrict reassignment** toggle and selecting a user or group from the drop-down.  
     ![](/migration-assets/configuring-actions-for-process-form-steps/5.png)
    
    > **Note**:
    > 
    > In the runtime, you can reassign an item only to one individual user part of the permitted app roles at a time.
    > 
    > ![](/migration-assets/configuring-actions-for-process-form-steps/4.png)
    
-   **Send** **back** allows the assignee to send the item back to a previous step in the workflow.
-   **Reject** sends the item back to the creator and cancels the workflow. Rejected items can be restarted, but are assigned a new request number. 

Hovering over the options allows you to show, hide, or customize these buttons and also add validations.

##### Initiator actions for items in progress

Once initiators submit an item, they can track it in the workflow through **My items**. When they open the item, they will see the fields configured at the **Start** step. At the bottom will be a set of actions they can take. You can configure these options under **Configure actions** and then select **Configure initiator actions**.

![](/migration-assets/configuring-actions-for-process-form-steps/7.png)

-   Reassign allows the creator to assign the task to a different assignee. 
-   Withdraw allows the creator to remove the item from the workflow. 

You have the same options to either show, hide, or customize these buttons, as well as setting up validations, such as only if the item was created by a particular user.

## [Restricting the option to send-back items](/t/g9h9qt6/configuring-actions-for-process-steps#restricting-the-option-to-send-back-items)

You can decide to limit the number of preceding steps in your workflow to which an item can be sent back from a particular step in your process. This option allows additional control over the workflow. To enable this, turn on the toggle button and select the steps from the dropdown menu; the current step’s send-back functionality will then be limited to these steps.

> **Note:**
> 
> When you disable this option, items can be sent to all preceding steps.
