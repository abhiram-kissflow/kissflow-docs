## Item notifications

The item notifications listed below are predefined by the system, based on which you can create new custom notifications and configure trigger conditions, recipients, and content of your choice. These predefined notifications cannot be deleted but can only be toggled on or off.

**[S.no](http://S.no).**

**Notification event**

**Trigger**

**Recipient**

1

Item assigned

When an item is assigned to a user

Assignee

2

Item completed

When an item completes its workflow

Initiator

3

Item rejected

When an item is rejected by a step assignee

Initiator

4

Item withdrawn by the initiator

When an item is withdrawn by an initiator

Assignee

5

Item reassigned

When an item is reassigned to a user

Assignee

6

Item breached its deadline

When an item breaches its deadline

Assignee

7

Item breached its deadline (notification alert)

When an item breaches its deadline

Configured in workflow steps. [Learn more](/t/60h9qm9/configuring-deadline)

8

Item approaching deadline (notification alert)

When an item approaches its deadline

Configured in workflow steps. [Learn more](/t/60h9qm9/configuring-deadline)

9

Item breached its deadline (escalation)

When an item breaches its deadline

Configured in workflow steps. [Learn more](/t/60h9qm9/configuring-deadline)

10

Item approaching deadline (escalation)

When an item approaches its deadline

Configured in workflow steps. [Learn more](/t/60h9qm9/configuring-deadline)

> **Note:**  
> Notifications for all process related steps that are sent to the respective user will be redirected to the delegated user (if [task delegation](/t/60h4ak1) is **ON**), but **Item completed** (S.no. 2 in the above table) notification alone will be received only by the requestor or initiator of the item, even if the initiator/requestor has delegated their task to another user **.**
