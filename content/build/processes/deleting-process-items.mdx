By default, all process items are saved forever in your account as soon as they are created as a draft. If you want to delete requests, here are a few ways to do it. 

## Deleting items as a user

Users can delete their own drafts. However, they cannot delete items after they have been submitted. To delete a draft, go to the process you want and click **Created by me**. Under **Draft**, hover over the item, select the **checkbox,** and click **Delete**. 

![](/migration-assets/deleting-process-items/2.gif)

## Delete items as a Process Admin

Only Process admins can delete items that are either in progress or completed. 

Click the **More options** button (![](/migration-assets/publishing-editing-duplicating-archiving-and-deleting-a-process/6.png)) at the top right > **Administration**. This page gives you information about [every item in your process](/t/60h9qtj/admin-actions-in-a-process). Process admins can make changes to any item. 

To delete an item, hover over it, click the checkbox at the left, and then click **Delete**. This will erase all the data associated with the item within your Kissflow account.

![](/migration-assets/deleting-process-items/1.png)

## Deleting test data

If you are just building your process, you may have a lot of test data that you want to delete. You can follow the same steps as above. 

However, a better option is to use the [Simulate option](/t/y4h9qty/simulate-workflow) when creating the workflow. This will let you quickly add data and see how it performs in the workflow. All the test data will be deleted when you click the **Reset** **data** button.
