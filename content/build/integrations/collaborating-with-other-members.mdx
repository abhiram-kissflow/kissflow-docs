Kissflow plans:

✓ Basic ✓ Enterprise

When you create an integration, you become its default member and can change its settings, like adding or removing other members.

To manage members, click **More options** > **Share** > **\+ Add members** button. You can add collaborators already in your Kissflow account to the respective integration.

![](/migration-assets/collaborating-with-other-members/1.gif)

To remove members from your integration, click the **Remove member** button to the left of the member's name. The collaborator will not be able to access or view the integration thereafter.

![](/migration-assets/collaborating-with-other-members/2.png)
