Your home page is your starting point after [signing in](/t/60h9qz9/signing-in-to-kissflow) to the platform. This page provides at-a-glance summary of items, workflows, and other key information to help you stay organized and productive. Below is a detailed guide to everything in your homepage.

## **Key features on the home page**

The top bar provides access to  global search, help icon, notifications and account profile.

![](/migration-assets/homepage-overview/1.png)

## **Global Search**

[Global search](/t/83h9qlq/global-search) is located prominently at the top center, this feature allows you to search for anything across the product from items, workflows, or apps. You can search by item name, or keywords to quickly locate what you need.

## **Help icon**

Found on the top-right corner, the help icon connects you to:

-   The **help documentation** for guidance on using the platform.
-   The **Kissflow community**, where you can interact with other users and share insights.

## User profile

Click on your profile picture on the top-right corner to access:

-   **[My settings](/category/my-settings)** to customize your profile details and account preferences.
-   **[Themes](/category/themes)** to change the look and feel of the platform.
-   **Sign out** to log out of the platform.  
     

## **Ask - Kissflow's AI agent**

Ask Kissflow AI Agent enables users to interact with workflow data using natural language and generate insights instantly. It eliminates the need for manual report building and makes analytics accessible to everyone.

-   Query workflow data using natural language
-   Generate reports instantly
-   Explore insights conversationally

#### Getting started

Ask is a smart agent, that can generate insights you want from your workflows, follow these easy steps to get started:

Step 1: Click **Ask** directly from your homepage.  
Step 2: Click **\+ Add context** to select relevant workflows from apps, process, board, and dataset to guide the AI in generating accurate responses. A maximum of 3 workflows can be selected.  
Step 3: Enter your query in natural language to retrieve insights or generate reports. The agent interprets your intent, identifies the relevant fields within the selected workflows, queries the database, and returns a response.  
 

![](/migration-assets/homepage-overview/2.gif)

> ****Note:**** You must add context, to begin a conversation. Only workflows where you are a Process Admin, Report Admin, or Data Admin are available for selection.

#### **Generating a report**

Use simple prompts to create [reports](/category/reports-in-analytics) instantly. When a report is generated, click **Edit and save** to take the report to Analytics report builder, where you can make further changes and save the report, which will then be available in **Analytics > Reports.**  
 

![](/migration-assets/homepage-overview/11.gif)

   
Ask provides a conversational experience where you can continue with follow-up questions without resetting the context. It retains up to 40 previous conversations as context, helping you to maintain continuity.

#### **Response and feedback system**

The agent provides a clear explanation of results with key highlights. When you click **Show details**, it helps you understand how the response was generated. **Sources** shows you the workflows used and fields referenced while **Query** shows the exact database query used to fetch answers to your input.

![](/migration-assets/homepage-overview/3.png)![](/migration-assets/homepage-overview/4.png)

You can quickly share feedback on each response using the thumbs up or thumbs down options. A thumbs up indicates that the response was helpful, while a thumbs down lets us know it didn’t meet your expectations. When you provide feedback, you’ll also have the option to share more details—such as whether the response was accurate, how fast it was, and any additional comments. This helps us continuously improve the quality and usefulness of the AI’s responses.  
 

![](/migration-assets/homepage-overview/5.png)

Click the **More options** button to visit the help documentation, or to clear conversation.  You can also expand the interface for a more focused view.  
 

![](/migration-assets/homepage-overview/7.gif)

## Default widgets on your homepage

By default, your homepage contains five widgets with fixed system fields as columns:

-   **Items in your queue** – Items assigned to you.
-   **Items created by you** – Items you have initiated.
-   **Recently accessed** – Items you opened most recently.
-   **Tagged comments** – Comments where you have been mentioned.
-   **My to-do** – Items you’ve marked as to-do.![](/migration-assets/homepage-overview/12.gif) 

### Marking items as to-do

Process and board items assigned to you include **Mark as to-do** and **Remove from to-do** options at the top right, as shown in the gif below. Alternatively, from **items in your queue** in your homepage, you can open any item and mark it as a to-do. Once marked, the item will appear under the **My to-do** widget for easier tracking and prioritization as shown in the gif below.  
 

![](/migration-assets/homepage-overview/8.gif)

## Customizing your homepage with widgets

✕ Basic ✓ Enterprise

You can customize your homepage by adding, removing, and arranging widgets to match the way you work. This makes it easier to keep important information within reach. To start customizing, select the **Customize** button at the top right of the homepage. There are 13 widgets you can choose from, some are standard widgets while  some are flow-specific. Flow-specific need to be configured as shown in the gif below. Basically this is what you do:

-   Click **Customize** to pick the widgets you want.
-   Arrange them neatly and click **Save** as shown in the gif.
-   **Reset** will take you back to your last saved dashboard setup.
-   Deleting all widgets will restore the default dashboard with 5 widgets.  
     ![](/migration-assets/homepage-overview/10.gif)   

### Standard widgets

These widgets come ready to use and don’t require configuration.

**Widgets**

**Description**

Items in your queue

Shows all items currently assigned to you.

Items in your queue - overdue

Shows items in your queue that have passed their due date.

Items in your queue - due today

Shows items in your queue that are due today.

Items created by you

Lists all items initiated by you.

Tagged comments

Displays comments where you have been tagged.

My to-do

Shows items you’ve marked as to-do from processes or boards assigned to you.

Recently accessed

Quick access to the items you opened most recently.

Access requests

Lists access requests awaiting your action.

Flow creation requests

Displays requests for creating new flows.

### Flow-specific widgets

These widgets need to be configured. For example, you can narrow down to items in your queue, items created by me, chart or table reports from a specific flow.

**Widgets**

**Description**

Items in your queue - specific flow

Shows items assigned to you from a chosen flow.

Items created by me - specific flow

Lists items you created in a specific flow.

Chart reports

Visual representation of reports from your flows.

Tabular reports

Tabular report views from your flows.

> **Note:** You can add up to 3 report widgets. In all 15 widgets can be placed on your homepage.

## Using AI auto suggest

Instead of setting up each widget manually, you can use AI auto suggest as shown in the gif to:

-   **Get suggestions**: Our AI analyzes and surfaces widgets that are most likely to benefit you, based on your activity and usage patterns. This saves you the effort of configuring widgets. You only need to review the options and place them where they fit best.
    
-   **Ask for widgets:** Type what you need, for example, _I need items created by me in employee confirmation_, and the AI will auto configure the flow-specific widgets. You just need to drag and drop.  
     
    
    ![](/migration-assets/homepage-overview/9.gif)

> **Note:** AI does not create new widgets. It only suggests from the available list of widgets (shown in the table above) and one step further gives you configured flow-specific widgets based on your request.
