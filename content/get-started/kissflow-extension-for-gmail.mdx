The **Kissflow for Gmail** extension lets you view and approve items assigned to you in Kissflow directly from your Gmail inbox, without switching tabs.

## Install the extension

1. Go to the [Kissflow for Gmail](https://chromewebstore.google.com/detail/kissflow-for-gmail/cieofhhbpplmgpdlebmoocmoecneaolc) listing on the Chrome Web Store.
2. Click **Add to Chrome**.
3. Click **Add extension** to confirm.

## Sign in to Kissflow from Gmail

1. Open **Gmail**. A Kissflow icon appears on the right navigation panel.
2. Click the **Kissflow** icon.
3. Enter the domain name of your Kissflow account and click **Submit**.

<Callout type="info">
  Already have another Kissflow account signed in? See [switch between accounts](#manage-connected-accounts) below.
</Callout>

## Approve or act on items

Once signed in, the extension shows two sections:

- **My tasks** — items you can approve instantly.
- **Tasks awaiting your action in Kissflow** — items that need your input before they can be approved.

**To approve an item instantly:**

1. Under **My tasks**, find the item.
2. Click **Approve**.

If you want to review the item before approving it, click **View task** to open it in your Kissflow account.

**To act on an item that needs your input:**

1. Under **Tasks awaiting your action in Kissflow**, find the item.
2. Click **Submit** to open it in your Kissflow account, where you can provide the required input.

## Manage connected accounts

You can connect and switch between multiple Kissflow accounts from the extension.

1. Click your account name under **My tasks** to return to the extension's login page.
2. Enter the domain name of another Kissflow account and click **Submit**.

![](/migration-assets/kissflow-extension-for-gmail/1.png)

<Callout type="info">
  Click the **Refresh** icon to reload tasks with the latest data from your Kissflow account, the **Close** icon to exit the widget, or the **Trash** icon to remove an account from the extension.
</Callout>
