# What is Kissflow?

Kissflow is a work platform that lets you build apps, automate workflows, and manage work — without writing code.

## Who is Kissflow for?

<PersonaNav />

## Key capabilities

- **Apps** — Build custom business applications with drag-and-drop pages, forms, and data models
- **Processes** — Design multi-step approval workflows with conditional routing and SLA tracking
- **Boards** — Track work items across statuses with Kanban, list, timeline, and gallery views
- **Integrations** — Connect to Google Workspace, Salesforce, Slack, SAP, and 40+ services
- **AI Builder** — Generate workflows, form fields, formulas, and custom components with AI
- **Analytics** — Create reports, dashboards, and data views across all your apps
