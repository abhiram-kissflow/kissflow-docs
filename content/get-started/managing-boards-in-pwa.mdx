# Managing boards in the PWA

Manage your [boards](/docs/build/boards/boards-overview) from anywhere using the Kissflow Progressive Web App (PWA). The **Home** dashboard has two widgets for working with board items:

- **My tasks** — lists items assigned to you.
- **My items** — lists items you created.

![Home dashboard with the My tasks and My items widgets](/migration-assets/managing-boards-in-pwa/1.png)
{/* TODO: verify screenshot matches current product UI */}

## Create an item

1. On the dashboard, tap the **New item** (![New item icon](/migration-assets/managing-boards-in-pwa/2.png)) button next to a board.
2. In the form that opens, fill in the details — assignee, due date, status, priority, and any other fields your board captures.
3. Tap **Submit**.

<Callout type="info">
  You can also get to a board's new-item form by tapping **Explore**, opening the board, and tapping the **New item** button there.
</Callout>

![Creating a new item from the dashboard](/migration-assets/managing-boards-in-pwa/5.gif)
{/* TODO: verify screenshot matches current product UI */}

## Browse items in a board

1. Tap **Explore**.
2. Tap a board to see its items.
3. Switch between tabs to filter the view:
   - **All** — every item in the board. This is the default tab.
   - **Assigned to me** — items assigned to you for action.

<Callout type="info">
  If you're an [initiator](/docs/build/boards/managing-board-roles-and-permissions) in the board, you also get an **Initiated by me** tab alongside **All**, pinned by default.
</Callout>

4. Tap the **Sort** button (![Sort icon](/migration-assets/managing-boards-in-pwa/3.png)) to sort the items shown in a tab.

![Sorting items in a board tab](/migration-assets/managing-boards-in-pwa/6.gif)
{/* TODO: verify screenshot matches current product UI */}

## Manage an item

1. Open an item from the board.
2. Tap the **More options** button (![More options icon](/migration-assets/managing-boards-in-pwa/4.png)).
3. Choose an action:
   - **Copy link** — copies a direct URL for the item that you can share with others.
   - **Watch** — [watch](/docs/use/items/collaborating) an item that isn't assigned to you to keep track of its progress.
   - **Clone** — creates a duplicate of the item.
   - **Delete** — removes the item.

![More options menu on an item showing Copy link, Watch, Clone, and Delete](/migration-assets/managing-boards-in-pwa/8.gif)
{/* TODO: verify screenshot matches current product UI */}

## Track items from My tasks and My items

1. On the dashboard, tap the **My tasks** widget to see items assigned to you, or the **My items** widget to review the progress of items you created.
2. Tap an item to open it. Any changes you make to its fields are auto-saved.

You can also add [notes](/docs/use/boards/notes-status-logs-and-activity-logs-in-boards) to collaborate with others on the item. Notes support @mentions, emojis, attachments, and other formatting options.
