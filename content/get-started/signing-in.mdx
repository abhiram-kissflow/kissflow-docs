# Signing in to Kissflow

<PlanBadge plans={["basic", "enterprise"]} />

## Prerequisites

- A Kissflow account (your admin sends an invitation email)
- A supported browser (see [Supported browsers](/docs/reference/supported-browsers))

## Sign in with email

1. Go to your Kissflow account URL (e.g., `yourcompany.kissflow.com`)
2. Enter your **email address**
3. Enter your **password**
4. Click **Sign In**

<Callout type="info" title="SSO">
  If your organization uses SSO, you may be redirected to your identity provider (Okta, Azure AD, Google) instead of seeing a password field.
</Callout>

## Sign in on mobile

1. Download the Kissflow app from the App Store or Google Play
2. Enter your account URL or scan the QR code from your desktop
3. Sign in with your credentials

## Troubleshooting

| Problem | Fix |
|---|---|
| "Account not found" | Check with your admin that your email was added to the account |
| Password reset | Click **Forgot password** on the sign-in page |
| SSO redirect loop | Clear browser cookies, try incognito mode, contact admin |
