Effective end of March, Kissflow discontinued the self-serve support option in the **Subscription & billing** section for the following plans:

- **KFDW** — Free, Basic, Advanced, Fully Loaded
- **KF WORKFLOW** — Basic, Advanced, Fully Loaded

## What changed

The **Manage plans** button, previously used to purchase additional user licenses directly, has been removed. A **Chat for help** button now appears under **Subscription details** in the **Subscription & billing** section. Use it to reach support for additional licenses, plan changes, or billing-frequency adjustments.
