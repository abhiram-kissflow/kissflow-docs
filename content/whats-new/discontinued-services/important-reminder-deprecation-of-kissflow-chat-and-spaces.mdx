# Important reminder: Deprecation of Kissflow Chat and Spaces

Effective **1 July 2024**, Kissflow Chat and Kissflow Spaces were discontinued.

## What changed

All functionality for both services was removed on this date, and access is no longer available.

## What to do

If you have questions about this change, contact Kissflow support.
