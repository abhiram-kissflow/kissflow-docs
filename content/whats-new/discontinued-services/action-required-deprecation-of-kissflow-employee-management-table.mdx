# Action required: Deprecation of Kissflow Employee Management Table

Effective **mid-January 2024**, Kissflow deprecated the Employee Management Table. After this date, employee details are no longer accessible through that table.

## What changed

All employee details previously available in the Employee Management Table are now accessible through the User Management table.

Access is more restricted than before: only User admins, IAM admins, and Super admins can view user details there. Previously, employee details could be shared with a wider set of users.

The `_employee()` system field is no longer available for new use. Existing fields that referenced `_employee()` were automatically replaced with `user()`. Use `user()` in new workflows going forward.

## What to do

If you were sharing employee details with other users through the Employee Management Table, let them know about this change and direct them to a User admin, IAM admin, or Super admin for anything they still need.

<Callout type="info">
  This change applies to accounts on the KF Digital Workflow, KF Process, KF Project, KF Workflow Lite, KF Workflow Suite, and KF Workflow plans.
</Callout>

If you have questions, contact Kissflow support.
