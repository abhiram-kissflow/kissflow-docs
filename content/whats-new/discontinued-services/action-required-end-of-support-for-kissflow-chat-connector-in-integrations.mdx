Effective 20 December 2023, Kissflow Integrations will no longer support the Kissflow Chat connector. Please be aware that any integrations dependent on Kissflow Chat will cease functioning after this date. 

We strongly recommend transitioning to alternative connectors within our system to ensure uninterrupted communication and workflows. You can easily configure your integrations with popular platforms such as Google Chat, Teams, and Slack.

We understand the critical role of uninterrupted communication in your business, and our dedicated support team is ready to assist you throughout this transition. If you have any concerns or questions, please feel free to comment on this post.
