## How to reach support

Kissflow Community is the primary channel for support. Access it in-product by clicking the **(?)** icon next to your profile picture, or go directly to [community.kissflow.com](https://community.kissflow.com/). Use the **Get Help** section to post questions or report issues and defects — this is the main channel Kissflow support monitors, and questions about using the product get a prompt response.

Live chat is a separate, secondary channel for account-specific issues or defects. Open it from the same **(?)** icon in the top navigation bar — it launches a standalone chat widget rather than a thread inside Community. Chat availability can vary depending on your product and plan.

<Callout type="info">
  Only use email ([support@kissflow.com](mailto:support@kissflow.com)) when you can't log in to Kissflow, for example during downtime. Emails are routed into the same support chat system and handled by support specialists.
</Callout>

## Status page

Kissflow's systems are actively monitored, and any issues are reported on the [Kissflow status page](https://status.kissflow.com/). Check this page first if you're seeing service availability issues or downtime.

## Chat response times

| Metric | Target |
| --- | --- |
| Conversations answered | Within 24 hours on business days |
| Average response time | About 30 minutes during business hours |

## Defect SLAs

Once a product specialist confirms a defect, the following SLAs apply based on severity. All timelines exclude Saturdays and Sundays.

| Severity | Example | Ticket response | Workaround | Permanent fix |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Critical | The system is down | 4 hours | 4 hours (immediate workaround\*) | 48 hours |
| Highly important | An entire module is affected | 4 hours | 24 hours | 1 week |
| Medium | A feature within a module is affected | 8 hours | 3 business days | Next release |
| Low | Annoyances | 48 hours | As seen fit | As seen fit |

\* An immediate workaround is an acceptable solution to the specific problem reported.

<Callout type="info">
  Enterprise customers with large deals can have these SLAs written into a signed contract. Contact [contact@kissflow.com](mailto:contact@kissflow.com) for details.
</Callout>
