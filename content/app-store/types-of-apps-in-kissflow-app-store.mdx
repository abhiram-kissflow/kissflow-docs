 Applications in the Kissflow App Store can be broadly classified as:

-   Installable apps
-   Provisional apps![](/migration-assets/types-of-apps-in-kissflow-app-store/4.png)   
    Once installed, all apps get deployed to your live account by default.

## Installable apps

This category in Kissflow App Store includes apps that users can directly install. These ready-made apps are entirely designed and developed by Kissflow specialists and are completely free to install.

![](/migration-assets/types-of-apps-in-kissflow-app-store/1.png)

## Provisional apps

These Kissflow App Store apps are customized specifically for Kissflow users based on their requests. When a user inquires about an app in Kissflow App Store, the Kissflow support staff contacts the user to understand the user's requirements better. The app is then modified to meet the needs of the user and deployed to the customer’s account.

![](/migration-assets/types-of-apps-in-kissflow-app-store/2.png)

If you don't find an application you require, you can fill out this form and send in a request with the application name and the features you are looking for.

![](/migration-assets/types-of-apps-in-kissflow-app-store/3.png)
