# Types of apps in Kissflow App Store

<PlanBadge plans={["enterprise"]} />

Apps in the Kissflow App Store fall into two categories: **Installable** and **Provisional**. Once installed, every app deploys directly to your live account by default.

| App type | How you get it | What it is |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Installable | Install directly from the app's card or landing page | A ready-made app you can use as-is |
| Provisional | Submit an enquiry; Kissflow customizes and deploys it to your account | An app tailored to your requirements before deployment |

## Installable apps

Installable apps are ready-made apps built by Kissflow that you can install directly — no enquiry or approval step required.

![Installable app card in the Kissflow App Store](/migration-assets/types-of-apps-in-kissflow-app-store/1.png)
{/* TODO: verify screenshot matches current product UI */}

## Provisional apps

Provisional apps are customized to a specific user's requirements rather than installed as-is. When you enquire about one, Kissflow's support team follows up to understand what you need, then modifies the app and deploys it to your account.

![Provisional app enquiry form asking for team size and desired features](/migration-assets/types-of-apps-in-kissflow-app-store/2.png)
{/* TODO: verify screenshot matches current product UI */}

## Request an app that isn't listed

If you don't find an app you need in the App Store, fill out the suggestion form with the app name and the features you're looking for.
