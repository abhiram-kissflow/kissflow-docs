 Kissflow App Store contains a variety of applications tailored to the needs of the user. Applications featured in the App Store can be installed directly, purchased, or requested. 

To access the Kissflow App Store, follow the below steps:

1.  Sign in to Kissflow.
2.  Click the **Kissflow App Store** button ( ![](/migration-assets/accessing-kissflow-app-store/1.png))
3.  Select and install the app of your choice.![](/migration-assets/accessing-kissflow-app-store/2.png)
