# Accessing the Kissflow App Store

<PlanBadge plans={["enterprise"]} />

The Kissflow App Store is a collection of pre-built apps for common business use cases. From the App Store, apps can be installed directly, purchased, or requested, depending on the app.

## Open the App Store

1. Sign in to Kissflow.
2. Click the **Kissflow App Store** icon.

   ![Kissflow App Store icon](/migration-assets/accessing-kissflow-app-store/1.png)
   {/* TODO: verify screenshot matches current product UI */}

3. Browse the apps on offer. Each app's card shows whether it can be installed, purchased, or requested.

   ![Kissflow App Store home page listing available apps](/migration-assets/accessing-kissflow-app-store/2.png)
   {/* TODO: verify screenshot matches current product UI */}

<Callout type="info">
  Only Super Admins can install an app. See [Using the Kissflow App Store](/docs/app-store/using-kissflow-app-store) for the install, purchase, and enquiry steps.
</Callout>
