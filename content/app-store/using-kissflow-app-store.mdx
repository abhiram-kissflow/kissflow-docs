# Using the Kissflow App Store

<PlanBadge plans={["enterprise"]} />

Apps in the Kissflow App Store are either installable — free, ready to use as soon as you install them — or provisional, meaning they need to be customized before they can be deployed to your account. This article covers both flows.

## Install an app

<Callout type="info">
  Only Super Admins can install an app.
</Callout>

1. Sign in to your Kissflow account.
2. Click the **Kissflow App Store** icon.

   ![Kissflow App Store icon](/migration-assets/using-kissflow-app-store/2.png)
   {/* TODO: verify screenshot matches current product UI */}

3. Hover over the app you want to install. The **Install** button appears on the app's card, or on its landing page if you open it.
4. Click **Install**, then **Confirm**.

<Callout type="warn">
  Once you confirm, the installation cannot be stopped.
</Callout>

The app deploys to your live account and is available from the App Store and the explorer.

<Callout type="info">
  You can install an app only once per account.
</Callout>

## Enquire about a provisional app

Provisional apps are customized to a user's requirements rather than installed as-is. When you enquire about one, Kissflow's support team follows up to understand what you need before deploying a version of the app to your account.

1. Sign in to your Kissflow account.
2. Click the **Kissflow App Store** icon.

   ![Kissflow App Store icon](/migration-assets/using-kissflow-app-store/2.png)
   {/* TODO: verify screenshot matches current product UI */}

3. Hover over an app card that shows an **Enquire** button, and click it to open the enquiry form.
4. Fill in the features you want and the size of your team.

   ![Enquiry form for a provisional app](/migration-assets/using-kissflow-app-store/1.png)
   {/* TODO: verify screenshot matches current product UI */}

5. Click **Submit**.

Kissflow's support staff will contact you regarding your enquiry.
