# Changing the background theme

<PlanBadge plans={["basic", "enterprise"]} />

You can switch the background theme of your Kissflow account between **System default**, **Light**, **Dark**, and **Dim** modes from the **Themes** section.

## Change your theme

1. Click your profile picture in the upper-right corner of the page.
2. Select **Themes**.

   ![Profile menu open with the Themes option highlighted](/migration-assets/changing-the-background-theme/1.png)
   {/* TODO: verify screenshot matches current product UI */}

3. Choose a theme. Your selection is applied immediately.
4. Click **Done** to close the dialog.

The available themes are:

- **System default** — matches the theme set on your local device or browser.
- **Light** — a white background with dark text.
- **Dark** — a near-black background with light text.
- **Dim** — a dark grey background with light text.

<Callout type="info">
  The theme is applied as soon as you select it — **Done** only closes the dialog, so you can preview a theme before dismissing it.
</Callout>

<Callout type="info">
  The theme you select applies to all Kissflow accounts you access from the same browser.
</Callout>
