# Managing active sessions

<PlanBadge plans={["basic", "enterprise"]} />

The Active sessions page lists every device your account is currently signed in from, along with the browser, operating system, and IP address used for each session. Use it to spot sessions you don't recognize and sign them out.

## Access active sessions

1. Sign in to Kissflow and click your profile picture in the upper-right corner of the home page.
2. Click **My settings** > **Security settings** > **Active sessions**.

## What each session shows

Every entry in the list is a single device or browser where your account is signed in.

| Detail | Description |
| --- | --- |
| Device information | The web browser and, for computer sessions, the operating system version. |
| IP address | The IP address the session is connecting from. |
| First signed in on | The date and time the session first signed in. Hover over it to see the exact timestamp. |

<Callout type="info">
  Operating system version is only shown for sessions from a computer. For other device types, this field is left blank.
</Callout>

## Sign out of a session

- **Sign out of one session** — click the **More options** button on that session and select **Sign out**. Signing out your current session prompts you to confirm before it signs you out.
- **Sign out of every session** — click **Sign out from all active sessions** to sign out of your current session and every other active session on all devices at once. This also asks for confirmation before it proceeds.

<Callout type="warn">
  Review this page regularly and sign out any session you don't recognize. If you spot an unfamiliar session, also change your password.
</Callout>
