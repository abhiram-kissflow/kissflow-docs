# Personal info, format settings, and task delegation

<PlanBadge plans={["basic", "enterprise"]} />

The **General settings** section of **My settings** is where you manage your own profile, password, display preferences, and task delegation — separate from account-wide settings, which admins manage.

## Access your settings

1. Click your profile picture in the upper-right corner of the Home screen.
2. Click **My settings**.

The **My settings** page has three categories: **General**, **Security**, and **Notification settings**. **General settings** contains **Personal info**, **Format settings**, and **Task Delegation**.

## Personal info

The **Personal info** page lets you fill in your details, upload a profile picture, add a signature, and manage your email address.

### Change your password

1. Click **Change password**.
2. Enter your current password and your new password.
3. Click **Save**.

### Reset a forgotten password

1. Click **Change password** > **Forgot password?**
2. Click **Send email**.

You'll receive an email with instructions to reset your password.

<Callout type="warn">
  The reset link in this email expires after 24 hours. If it expires before you use it, restart the **Forgot password?** flow to get a new one.
</Callout>

## Format settings

Format settings control how data and time-based values are displayed for your own account. They're separate from the account-wide format settings, which are typically managed by admins with account administration permissions (see [Super Admin](/docs/admin/users/user-roles-and-permissions)).

- **Account time zone** — used when a scheduled action in an integration runs, or when a deadline in a workflow is evaluated. Data from your processes, boards, and apps is displayed in this time zone.
- **Language** — the display language for your account. Kissflow supports more than 20 languages, including English, French, Spanish, German, Italian, Russian, Korean, Simplified Chinese, Japanese, Vietnamese, Thai, Polish, Portuguese, Slovak, Czech, Ukrainian, Hindi, and Romanian. Availability of some languages, such as Arabic and Persian, may depend on your account's configuration.
- **Date format** — the display format for date fields across your account.

<Callout type="info">
  Number and currency format aren't configurable — they're always based on your browser's locale settings.
</Callout>

Click **Save** after choosing your preferences.

## Task delegation

Task delegation automatically reassigns your process tasks to another user while you're unavailable. Items already in your queue when you set up the delegation stay with you — only new items sent to you are redirected to the delegatee.

1. Click **Add delegation**.
2. Under **Who do you want to delegate your tasks to?**, select a user.
3. Specify how long you want your items delegated to that user.

![Task delegation setup showing the delegatee and duration fields](/migration-assets/personal-info-format-settings-and-task-delegation/2.png)
{/* TODO: verify screenshot matches current product UI */}

<Callout type="info">
  Existing process items in your queue aren't delegated. Only new process items are redirected to the delegatee.
</Callout>

### Edit or cancel a delegation

- **Edit** — click **Edit delegation** beside the delegated user's name and update the details.
- **Cancel** — click **Cancel delegation**. Kissflow resumes assigning new items to you as usual, but items already delegated remain with the delegated user.

![Delegation list showing Edit delegation and Cancel delegation actions](/migration-assets/personal-info-format-settings-and-task-delegation/3.png)
{/* TODO: verify screenshot matches current product UI */}

<Callout type="info">
  A Super Admin can override any delegation you set. Once a delegation has been overridden, a Super Admin can't override it a second time, but they can still edit their existing override.
</Callout>
