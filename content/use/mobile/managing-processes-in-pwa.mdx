# Managing processes in the PWA

The dashboard in the Kissflow Progressive Web App (PWA) has two widgets for working with process items: **My tasks** and **My items**.

## My tasks

A task is an item that's awaiting your approval or some other action. The **My tasks** widget on the **Home** page shows your ongoing and overdue tasks, and you can add filters based on due date. Tap **Show all** in the **My tasks** section to open a detailed list of all your tasks.

### Managing approvals

1. Tap the **My tasks** widget on the dashboard.
2. Tap a task from the list.
3. Tap **Approve** to approve the item.
4. Tap **More actions** to perform another action instead. From here you can reject, send back, or reassign the item.

   <Callout type="info">
     Withdraw isn't available from **More actions** on a task. Withdrawing an item is only available to the person who initiated it, from the **My items** widget.
   </Callout>

   ![Task action sheet showing Approve and More actions options](/migration-assets/managing-processes-in-pwa/1.gif)

### Other actions

1. Tap the **My tasks** widget on the dashboard.
2. Tap a task card from the list.
3. Tap the **More options** menu in the upper-right corner of the task.
4. Tap **Progress** to view the workflow and the task's current status.
5. Tap the **Comments** button (![Comments icon](/migration-assets/managing-processes-in-pwa/2.png)) to leave a comment about the task.

   ![Progress and Comments views opened from a task's More options menu](/migration-assets/managing-processes-in-pwa/3.gif)
   {/* TODO: verify screenshot matches current product UI */}

<Callout type="info">
  If you don't have any tasks assigned yet, you can still stay updated by [enabling push notifications](/docs/use/mobile/android-devices#enabling-push-notifications).
</Callout>

## My items

The **My items** widget shows the items you've initiated. You can filter them by status and creation timeline.

1. Tap the **My items** widget on the dashboard.
2. Tap an item card from the list.
3. Tap **Withdraw** or **Reassign**, based on what you want to do.
4. Tap the **More options** menu in the upper-right corner of the item to view its progress or duplicate it.
