# iOS devices

On an iOS device, you can access your Kissflow account either through a mobile browser or by installing it as a Progressive Web App (PWA).

## Use a mobile browser

Open your preferred mobile browser and go to your account URL: `<accountname>.kissflow.com`.

## Use a Progressive Web App (PWA)

A Progressive Web App gives you a native app-like experience — it looks and feels like a mobile app, consumes less data than the browser experience, and updates itself in the background without requiring any action from you.

<Callout type="info">
  For the best experience, use a device with at least 2 GB of RAM.
</Callout>

## Install the PWA on iOS

1. Open **Safari**.
2. Type your account URL in the address bar — `https://<accountname>.kissflow.com`.
3. Tap the **Actions** (share) icon.
4. Tap **Add to Home Screen**.
5. Tap **Add**.

<Callout type="warn">
  iOS prevents Chrome from adding a PWA to the home screen. Use Safari to install the PWA.
</Callout>
