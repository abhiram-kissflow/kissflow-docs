# Android devices

On Android, you can access your Kissflow account through your mobile browser, or install Kissflow as a **Progressive Web App (PWA)** for a more app-like experience.

## Using a mobile browser

Open your preferred mobile browser and go to your account's URL: `<accountname>.kissflow.com`.

## Using a Progressive Web App (PWA)

A Progressive Web App gives you a native app-like experience directly from your browser, without installing anything from the Play Store.

<Callout type="info">
  Recommended minimum: 2 GB of RAM on your device.
</Callout>

New features are added to the PWA on an ongoing basis. At any given time, it may support a smaller feature set than the full desktop experience.

### Advantages of the PWA

- **Faster updates** — the PWA loads the latest version from the browser each time you use it, so you're not waiting on a staged rollout like you would with a native app update.
- **Nothing to install** — it runs in your mobile browser instead of installing as a separate app from the Play Store.

## Installing the PWA

1. On your Android device, open **Chrome**, **Firefox**, or another browser and go to your Kissflow account URL: `https://<accountname>.kissflow.com`.

   ![Kissflow login page opened in a mobile browser](/migration-assets/android-devices/1.jpg)
   {/* TODO: verify screenshot matches current product UI */}

2. Tap the browser's menu button (![Browser menu icon](/migration-assets/android-devices/3.png)) and select **Add to Home screen**.
   {/* TODO: verify screenshot matches current product UI */}
3. Tap **Add**.

The Kissflow icon appears on your device's home screen, and you can open it just like any other app.

## Enabling push notifications

1. Tap your profile picture in the upper-right corner.
2. Tap the **More options** button (![More options icon](/migration-assets/android-devices/3.png)).
3. Tap **Notification settings**.
4. Toggle on **Push notifications**.

   ![Notification settings screen with the Push notifications toggle enabled](/migration-assets/android-devices/2.jpg)
