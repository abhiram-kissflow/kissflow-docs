# Creating and managing subitems

<PlanBadge plans={["basic", "enterprise"]} />

Subitems help you break down complex tasks into smaller pieces and track them efficiently. Like an item, a subitem has an assignee, a state, a start date, and a due date. Breaking down items into subitems helps you estimate the scope and duration of a task, which can significantly improve turnaround time.

## Create a subitem

1. Open the item from the board and click **Subitems** in the right navigation strip.
2. Click **Add subitem**.
3. Type a title for the subitem.

You can also create subitems in other ways:

- **Paste text** — copy a list of text and click the **Paste subitems** button inside the item. You can paste up to 20 subitems at a time.
- **Generate with AI** — let AI suggest subitems related to your main item, then click **Generate** to create them.

<Callout type="info">
  An item can have up to **100 subitems**.
</Callout>

To rename a subitem, hover over it and click the **Edit title** button, or open the subitem and rename it in its form.

## Manage a subitem

- **Set the state** — choose a state from the dropdown to indicate progress: **Not started** (the default), **In progress**, **On hold**, or **Done**.
- **Set an assignee** — click the **Set an assignee** button and choose a user. Restricted members can be assigned to a subitem only if they're the assignee of the item.
- **Set dates** — click the **Set start and due dates** button and select the dates.
- **Copy the URL** — click the **More options** button > **Copy URL** to share or reference the subitem.
- **Clone** — click the **More options** button > **Clone**. The clone inherits all the properties and information of the original.
- **Delete** — click the **More options** button > **Delete**.
- **Reorder** — drag and drop subitems into the positions you want.

## Find subitems in a board

- **Search** — enter the name of the subitem.
- **Expand or collapse** — use the **Expand subitems** / **Collapse subitems** button to show or hide all subitems in the board, or expand a single item to see just its subitems.
- **Filter** — enable **Apply filter to subitems** in the filter pane to include subitems in your filtered results.

## Convert and move subitems

Sometimes a subitem grows into work that needs to be tracked separately, or scattered items belong under a common parent. You can convert in both directions, and move subitems between items.

### Convert a subitem to an item

Click the **More options** button on the subitem > **Convert to item**. In the dialog, map the title (mandatory) and description (optional) of the subitem to fields in the board form, then click **Continue**.

![Animation of converting a subitem into an item by mapping its title and description fields](/migration-assets/creating-and-managing-subitems/18.gif)
{/* TODO: verify screenshot matches current product UI */}

### Convert an item to a subitem

Open the item, click the **More options** button at the top right of the form, and select **Convert to subitem**. Select the target item, map the title and description fields, and click **Continue**. If you convert an item that has subitems, the item and its subitems all become subitems of the selected item.

![Animation of converting an item into a subitem of another item](/migration-assets/creating-and-managing-subitems/16.gif)
{/* TODO: verify screenshot matches current product UI */}

### Move a subitem to another item

Click the **More options** button on the subitem > **Move to other item**, then select the target item.

![Animation of moving a subitem from one item to another](/migration-assets/creating-and-managing-subitems/17.gif)
{/* TODO: verify screenshot matches current product UI */}

<Callout type="info">
  If an item's assignee is a restricted member, only items assigned to that restricted member are listed when an admin or member converts items to subitems or moves subitems.
</Callout>
