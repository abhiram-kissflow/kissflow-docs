# Weekends and holidays

<PlanBadge plans={["basic", "enterprise"]} />

As a [Super Admin](/docs/admin/users/user-roles-and-permissions), you can set your organization's workweek and holidays so they're factored in when [deadlines](/docs/build/processes/workflow-design/configuring-deadline) and SLAs are calculated for workflow activities.

## Set your organization's weekend

1. Click your profile picture in the top right corner and go to **Account administration** > **Account settings** > **Weekends and holidays**.
2. Under **Weekends**, click each day your organization doesn't work to select it.

<Callout type="info">
  No weekend days are selected by default. Set your weekend here before it's needed for deadline or SLA calculations.
</Callout>

## Add office holidays

1. Click **Add holiday**.
2. Select the date.
3. Give the holiday a name.

![Weekends and holidays settings page showing the weekly working days and holiday list](/migration-assets/weekends-and-holidays/1.png)
{/* TODO: verify screenshot matches current product UI */}

<Callout type="warn">
  Holidays don't recur automatically. Your holiday list is for a single year, so you'll need to add next year's holidays yourself when the time comes.
</Callout>

## How this affects deadlines and SLAs

Weekend days and holidays you've configured here are excluded from [deadline](/docs/build/processes/workflow-design/configuring-deadline) and SLA calculations for workflow activities. If part of a task's allotted time falls on a non-working day, that day isn't counted — the deadline extends to give the assignee the full amount of working time you intended.
