# Format settings

<PlanBadge plans={["basic", "enterprise"]} />

As a Super Admin — or any admin granted the Format Settings permission — you control your account's default time zone, language, and date format. These settings determine how dates, times, and flow data are displayed across your organization.

<Callout type="info">
  Individual users can override the account's time zone, date format, and language for their own account from **My settings**. See [Personal info, format settings, and task delegation](/docs/use/personal-settings/personal-info-format-settings-and-task-delegation).
</Callout>

## Update format settings

1. Click your profile picture in the upper-right corner of the Home screen.
2. Click **Account administration** > **Format settings**.
3. Set your account's time zone, language, and date format (details below).
4. Click **Save**.

### Time zone

Your organization's time zone is used when a scheduled action in an integration runs, or when a [deadline](/docs/build/processes/workflow-design/configuring-deadline) in a workflow is evaluated — all flow data is matched to this time zone.

Select a time zone directly from the dropdown, or use the map below it: click a region to select its time zone, and use the zoom controls to find smaller zones more precisely.

### Language

Choose the display language for your account. Kissflow supports more than 20 languages, including English, French, Spanish, German, Italian, Russian, Korean, Simplified Chinese, Japanese, Vietnamese, Thai, Polish, Portuguese, Slovak, Czech, Ukrainian, Hindi, and Romanian.

<Callout type="info">
  Arabic is the exception — it's only available if your account has been enabled for it (see [Languages supported by Kissflow](/docs/whats-new/important-notices/languages-supported-by-kissflow)).
</Callout>

### Date format

Choose how date and datetime fields are displayed across your account:

- **Local** — based on your browser settings.
- **US** — M/D/YYYY.
- **European** — D/M/YYYY.
- **ISO** — YYYY-MM-DD.

<Callout type="info">
  Date format only affects date and datetime fields. Number and currency formatting is separate — see below.
</Callout>

### Number and currency format

Number and currency formatting is always based on the browser's local conventions and isn't configurable from this page.
