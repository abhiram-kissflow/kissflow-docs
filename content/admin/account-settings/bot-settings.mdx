# Bot settings

<PlanBadge plans={["enterprise"]} />

As a Super Admin, you can enable the **Kissflow Bot** for your organization's chat app — Slack, Microsoft Teams, or Google Chat. Once enabled, members can sign in to Kissflow from the chat app and receive real-time notifications about their processes and boards without switching tabs, then act on them (view, approve, reject, add notes) right from the chat.

<iframe src="https://player.vimeo.com/video/1076012619?app_id=122963" title="Kissflow x Chat apps - Overview video" className="aspect-video w-full rounded-lg border" allowFullScreen />

## Enable the Kissflow Bot

1. Click your profile picture in the top right corner and go to **Account administration** > **Account settings** > **Bot settings**.
2. Turn on the toggle for each chat app your organization uses: **Google Chat**, **Slack**, or **Microsoft Teams**.

![Bot settings page with toggles for Google Chat, Slack, and Microsoft Teams](/migration-assets/bot-settings/1.png)
{/* TODO: verify screenshot matches current product UI */}

Once a chat app is turned on, members can sign in to it using the steps below.

## Sign in to Kissflow from Google Chat

1. Install the Kissflow app:
   1. Go to the [Google Workspace Marketplace](https://workspace.google.com/marketplace?pann=ogb).
   2. Find the **Kissflow** app and install it.

   ![Kissflow app listing in the Google Workspace Marketplace](/migration-assets/bot-settings/3.png)
   {/* TODO: verify screenshot matches current product UI */}

2. Look for **Kissflow** in your Google Chat list.
3. Sign in to your Kissflow account:
   1. Type **/login** inside the Kissflow app and press **Enter** to sign in. A login URL is generated automatically.
   2. Click the login URL, enter your Kissflow account domain, and click **Submit**.
   3. Click **Allow** in the permissions popup that appears.

   ![Permissions popup for signing in to Kissflow from Google Chat](/migration-assets/bot-settings/2.png)
   {/* TODO: verify screenshot matches current product UI */}

Once signed in, you'll start receiving notifications from your processes and boards directly in the Kissflow app within Google Chat. To sign out, type **/logout** and press **Enter**.

## Sign in to Kissflow from Microsoft Teams

<iframe src="https://player.vimeo.com/video/1121104456?app_id=122963" title="Kissflow App for Microsoft Teams" className="aspect-video w-full rounded-lg border" allowFullScreen />

1. Install the Kissflow app:
   1. On Microsoft Teams, click **Apps** in the left sidebar.
   2. Find the **Kissflow** app and add it.

   ![Kissflow app in the Microsoft Teams Apps list](/migration-assets/bot-settings/4.png)
   {/* TODO: verify screenshot matches current product UI */}

2. Open your newly added Kissflow chat.
3. Sign in to your Kissflow account:
   1. Type **login** (no slash) inside the Kissflow app and press **Enter** to sign in. A login URL is generated automatically.
   2. Click the login URL, enter your Kissflow account domain, and click **Submit**.
   3. Click **Allow** in the permissions popup that appears.

   ![Permissions popup for signing in to Kissflow from Microsoft Teams](/migration-assets/bot-settings/5.png)
   {/* TODO: verify screenshot matches current product UI */}

Once signed in, you'll start receiving notifications from your processes and boards directly in the Kissflow app chat within Microsoft Teams. To sign out, type **logout** and press **Enter**.

## Sign in to Kissflow from Slack

1. Install the Kissflow app:
   1. On Slack, click **Apps**.
   2. Find the **Kissflow** app and add it.
2. Open your newly added Kissflow chat.
3. Sign in to your Kissflow account:
   1. Type **/login** inside the Kissflow app and press **Enter** to sign in. A login URL is generated automatically.
   2. Click the login URL, enter your Kissflow account domain, and click **Submit**.
   3. Click **Allow** in the permissions popup that appears.

Once signed in, you'll start receiving notifications from your processes and boards directly in the Kissflow app chat within Slack. To sign out, type **/logout** and press **Enter**.

## Notification actions

Members can act on a notification directly from the card that appears in their chat app:

| Module  | Available actions                    |
| ------- | ------------------------------------- |
| Process | View item, Approve item, Reject item  |
| Board   | View item, Add note                   |

## Notification events

Notifications from processes and boards are sent to the chat app for the following events:

| Module  | Event                     | Recipient            |
| ------- | ------------------------- | --------------------- |
| Process | Item assigned to a user   | Assignee               |
| Process | Item approved             | Item initiator         |
| Process | Item rejected              | Item initiator         |
| Process | Item reassigned           | Newly assigned user    |
| Process | Item deadline breached     | Assignee               |
| Board   | Item assigned to a user   | Assignee               |
| Board   | Item deadline breached     | Assignee               |
| Board   | User mentioned in a note   | Mentioned user          |

<Callout type="info">
  The events and recipients above reflect standard notification behavior. Your organization's [notification settings](/docs/use/personal-settings/user-notification-settings) can further customize which events are sent for specific processes, boards, or apps.
</Callout>
