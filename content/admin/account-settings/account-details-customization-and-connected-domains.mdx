## Account details

### Account name and domain

Your account name and domain are automatically assigned based on the email domain used to sign up. You can change this domain anytime if another organization doesn't take the new domain.

If you change the domain address yourself, Kissflow will automatically redirect the old domain to the new one, and all domain users will be locked out and notified. However, your former domain will become available to register by another organization. If it is claimed by someone else, your old domain will no longer redirect.

### Account owner

The individual responsible for setting up your Kissflow account is the Account Owner and acts as the main point of contact in case we need to contact you. Only one owner is allowed per account, but you can transfer ownership to another Super Admin if needed.  
  
Please reach out to Kissflow Support for this and the team will help you with the ownership transfer.

## Account customization

### Account logo 

Your account logo is auto-assigned based on the email domain used to sign up. It will appear on the top right corner of your account for all your users, sign-in screen, and certain product emails. You can also change it to a customized logo. 

We recommend using a rectangular logo that is 240x36 pixels. The logo file must be less than 250 KB and in JPG, PNG, or SVG format.

### Mobile app branding

You can also customize the branding for the Kissflow mobile app or PWA (Progressive Web App). Enter your own name of choice in the **App name** field. Use a custom logo for the mobile app by clicking the **Replace icon** button.

We recommend that you use a logo that is 512x512 pixels in resolution. The logo must be less than 500 KB in size and should be in PNG format.

### Account theme

You can apply your brand color to the header texts, links, and backgrounds in your account with the help of the **Account customization** settings.

![](/migration-assets/account-details-customization-and-connected-domains/3.png)

1.  Click your profile picture > **Account administration** > **Account settings** > **Account customization.**
2.  Under **Theme settings,** pick your brand color from the palette or enter its hex code as necessary.
    -   You can also apply a relevant color related to your brand color to the background if you wish to. You can enable the toggle button **Apply relevant changes to the background** field to **Yes** to update the background.
3.  Under the **Select a typeface** dropdown, you will find a variety of fonts to choose from. Once you apply, the selected font will be applied across the Kissflow account.
4.  Click **Preview** to check whether the applied brand color and typeface matches your expectations and whether the readability of text on the screen is good.
    -   You can also save the theme directly while previewing your preferred account theme by clicking **Save** or click **Discard changes** to revisit the section and modify your brand color as and when needed.

If, for some reason, you like the default color better, you can click **Reset to default** to reset the changes applied.

> **Note:**
> 
> We recommend you preview the theme before saving your preference because lighter shades might affect the readability of text on the screen.

##  Connected domains

For an organization with multiple email domains, Kissflow allows you to connect an email address from any of these domains to your account. Once an email domain is connected with your account, no one else can sign up for a new Kissflow account using the same domain.

Let’s say your organization’s domain address is _[starkcorporation.co](http://starkcorporation.co)_. If you want to connect a user from the same organization but with a different email domain _[stark-usa.co](http://stark-usa.co)_, you can do so by adding the new domain. Click **Connect a domain**, and then enter the domain name, domain address, and the email used to verify the domain.

![](/migration-assets/account-details-customization-and-connected-domains/2.gif)

An invite email will be sent to the invitee’s email inbox. Upon verification, the alternate domain will be connected to your account, and no one else can create a new Kissflow account with that domain. You can connect multiple domains with your Kissflow account. However, once connected, a domain cannot be associated with any other Kissflow account.
