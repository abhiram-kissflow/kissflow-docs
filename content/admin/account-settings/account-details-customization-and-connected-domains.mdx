# Account details, customization, and connected domains

<PlanBadge plans={["basic", "enterprise"]} />

Admins can view and manage these settings from your profile picture (top right of the home page) > **Account administration** > **Account settings**. From here you can view your account name and domain, customize your logo and theme, brand the mobile app, and connect additional email domains to your account.

<Callout type="info">
  Access to Account settings depends on your [admin role](/docs/admin/users/user-roles-and-permissions). Roles with account-level administration access can reach this page.
</Callout>

## Account details

### Account name and domain

Your account name and domain are automatically assigned based on the email domain you signed up with. You can change the domain at any time, as long as no other Kissflow organization is already using it.

To change your domain:

1. Go to your profile picture > **Account administration** > **Account settings** > **Account details**.
2. Click the domain field and enter your new domain.
3. Confirm the change.

<Callout type="warn">
  Changing your domain logs everyone out of their current session, including you. You're redirected to the new domain shortly after; other users need to navigate to the new domain themselves to sign back in — share the new domain with your team before you switch. Once you move off your old domain, it becomes available for another organization to claim.
</Callout>

### Account owner

The Account Owner is the person responsible for setting up your Kissflow account and is Kissflow's main point of contact for the account. Only one owner is allowed per account, but the current owner can transfer ownership to another admin at any time.

To transfer ownership:

1. Go to **Account settings** > **Account details**.
2. Next to **Account Owner**, click **Change**.

   <Callout type="info">
     Only the current Account Owner sees this option.
   </Callout>

3. Search for and select the new owner from the admin list.
4. Confirm the change.

## Account customization

### Account logo

Your account logo is auto-assigned based on the email domain used to sign up, and appears in the top right of your account, on the sign-in screen, and in certain product emails. You can replace it with your own.

1. Go to **Account settings** > **Account customization**.
2. Click **Replace logo** (or **Upload a logo** if none is set).
3. Choose your image file.

<Callout type="info">
  Use a rectangular logo, ideally 240x36 pixels. The file must be under 250 KB and in JPG, PNG, or SVG format.
</Callout>

### Mobile app branding

Customize how the Kissflow mobile app (or PWA) looks for your organization.

1. Under **Account customization**, enter a name in the **App name** field.
2. Click **Replace icon** (or **Upload an icon** if none is set) and choose your image file.

<Callout type="info">
  Use an icon that's 512x512 pixels, under 500 KB, and in PNG format.
</Callout>

### Account theme

Apply your brand color to header text, links, and backgrounds across your account, and choose an account-wide typeface.

1. Go to your profile picture > **Account administration** > **Account settings** > **Account customization**.
2. Under **Theme settings**, pick your brand color from the palette or enter its hex code.
   - Toggle **Apply relevant changes to the background** to also carry the color into background elements.
3. Under **Select a typeface**, choose a font. Once applied, it's used across your entire Kissflow account.
4. Click **Preview** to check how the color and typeface look before committing.
   - Click **Save** to keep the theme, or **Discard changes** to go back and adjust it.
   - To undo a color or font change, click the reset icon next to it and confirm in the **Reset to default value** popover.

<Callout type="info">
  Preview your theme before saving — lighter shades can affect text readability.
</Callout>

<Callout type="info">
  Theme settings and mobile app branding are only available if Kissflow has enabled these features for your account. If you don't see these sections, contact Kissflow support.
</Callout>

## Connected domains

If your organization uses multiple email domains, you can connect additional domains to your account. Once a domain is connected, no one else can sign up for a new Kissflow account using it.

For example, if your organization's domain is `starkcorporation.co` and you also use `stark-usa.co`, you can connect the second domain to the same account:

1. Click **Add a domain**.
2. Enter the domain name, domain address, and an email address on that domain to verify it.
3. Submit the request.

An invite email is sent to that address. Once verified, the domain is connected to your account, and no one else can use it to create a new Kissflow account.

<Callout type="info">
  You can connect multiple domains to one account, but each domain can only ever belong to a single Kissflow account.
</Callout>
