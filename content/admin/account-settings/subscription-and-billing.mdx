# Subscription and billing

<PlanBadge plans={["basic", "enterprise"]} />

Your account's subscription details — plan status, user license count, and invoices — are all available from Account administration.

<Callout type="info">
  Only Super Admins and Billing Admins can access this page. Learn more about [roles in Kissflow](/docs/admin/users/user-roles-and-permissions).
</Callout>

## View your subscription details

1. Click your profile picture in the top-right corner and select **Account administration**.
2. Go to **Subscription & billing**.

   ![Subscription and billing page showing plan status and user license details](/migration-assets/subscription-and-billing/1.png)
   {/* TODO: verify screenshot matches current product UI */}

This page shows the current status of your plan and the number of user licenses available under it.

<Callout type="info">
  Kissflow offers several plan tiers beyond the basics. To purchase additional licenses, change plans, or adjust your billing frequency, click **Chat for help** under **Subscription details** — see [Deprecation of self-serve option in Kissflow subscription & billing](/docs/whats-new/discontinued-services/important-announcement-deprecation-of-self-serve-option-in-kissflow-subscription-billing).
</Callout>

## View and download an invoice

1. Go to **Account administration** > **Subscription & billing**.
2. Click **View invoice**.

   ![Subscription and billing page with the View invoice action](/migration-assets/subscription-and-billing/2.png)
   {/* TODO: verify screenshot matches current product UI */}

From here, you can view and download the invoice for your account.
