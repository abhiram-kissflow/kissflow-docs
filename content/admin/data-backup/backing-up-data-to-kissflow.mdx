# Backing up data to Kissflow

<PlanBadge plans={["enterprise"]} />

<Callout type="info">
  Data backup is available on request. Contact Kissflow support to enable it on your account.
</Callout>

This is the simplest of Kissflow's backup destinations: instead of authenticating a cloud storage account, you choose **Email** as the storage mode and receive a downloadable link once the backup completes. As a [Super Admin](/docs/admin/users/user-roles-and-permissions#super-admin), you configure preferences once and reuse them for every future backup.

<Callout type="warn">
  You can only back up your data once every six months, and a backup can't be canceled once it starts. Plan your configuration and timing accordingly.
</Callout>

## Start data backup configuration

1. Sign in to Kissflow.
2. Click your profile picture at the top right of the page, then click **Account administration** > **Data backup**.
3. Click **Start configuration** on the **Data backup** page.

Configuration involves three steps: authenticating a storage mode, managing backup settings, and selecting data categories.

## Select email as your storage mode

1. Select **Email** as the storage mode from the dropdown.

   ![Data backup storage mode dropdown with Email selected](/migration-assets/backing-up-data-to-kissflow/1.png)
   {/* TODO: verify screenshot matches current product UI */}

2. Click **Next**.

<Callout type="info">
  After the backup completes, you'll receive a downloadable link by email. Download the file within seven days of receiving that email — the link expires after that.
</Callout>

## Manage backup settings

1. **Select ZIP file size.** Choose **4 GB**, **8 GB**, **16 GB**, or **24 GB**. Files in your account are automatically zipped and split according to your chosen limit.

   For example, a 24 GB limit applied to a 100 GB export produces four 24 GB files and one 4 GB file. Files 24 GB or smaller upload unchanged.

   ![ZIP file size selection dropdown](/migration-assets/backing-up-data-to-kissflow/2.png)
   {/* TODO: verify screenshot matches current product UI */}

2. **Encrypt backup file** (optional). Switch on this toggle to add a passphrase that encrypts your backed-up files for additional security. Your passphrase must contain:
   - At least eight characters.
   - Both upper and lower case characters.
   - At least one numeric character or symbol.

   <Callout type="warn">
     Save your passphrase somewhere secure. You'll need it to decrypt and access your backed-up files later, and it can't be recovered if lost.
   </Callout>

3. Click **Save**.

## Select data categories for backup

Choose one or more categories to include:

- **User and group data** — all account user and group data.
- **Transactional data** — flow item details such as status, comments, and members involved.
- **Audit log data** — the last year's audit logs at both the flow and account levels.
- **Attachments** — attachments across flows, including processes and boards.

Select your categories and click **Next**. In the confirmation popup, verify your configuration details and click **Start backup**.

![Backup confirmation popup showing configuration details and the Start backup button](/migration-assets/backing-up-data-to-kissflow/3.png)
{/* TODO: verify screenshot matches current product UI */}

<Callout type="info">
  Not ready to start? Click **Save and exit** to store your configuration and resume it later instead of running the backup immediately.
</Callout>

From the **Data backup** home page, you can also edit your configuration, delete it, or view your backup history.
