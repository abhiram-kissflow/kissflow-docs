✕ Basic ✓ Enterprise

As a [Super Admin, or an IAM Admin](/t/35h4ak0/user-roles-and-permissions), account governance provides you a clear view of all groups within your Kissflow account. It presents a list of groups, their creators, and the tally of members and subgroups they encompass. Selecting a group from the list directs you to a detailed page showcasing all users and subgroups within that group.This functionality is crucial in executing effective group governance, ensuring proper access controls are in place. It's a proactive measure to guarantee that sensitive resources within your account are accessed solely by authorized groups.
